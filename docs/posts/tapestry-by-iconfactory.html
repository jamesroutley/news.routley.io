Tapestry is a free app with premium features.

  • The web your way.

    Tapestry combines posts from your favorite social media services like Bluesky, Mastodon, Tumblr and others with RSS feeds, podcasts, YouTube channels and more. All of your content presented in chronological order, with no algorithm deciding what you should or shouldn't see.

  • Your timeline is your own business.

    Browsing your timeline in Tapestry is totally private. There's no server tracking what you're loading and no third-parties watching as you scroll or search through content. Even our ads are private!

  • Avoid Spoilers. Everywhere.

    Quickly create powerful rules that control what you do and don't see across all of your feeds. Avoid potential spoilers, stay clear of unwanted topics and more by creating muffle and mute rules that apply across every feed you follow.

  • Universal app

    Tapestry has native support for both iOS and iPadOS.

  • iCloud sync

    Sync your reading position across all your devices.

  • Fully accessible

    VoiceOver support, Dynamic Type, and more.

  • Customizable

    Change Tapestry's app icon, layout, and fonts.

  • Read later

    Mark items and come back to them later.

  • Light and dark mode

    Outside or inside, Tapestry is easy on the eyes.

Get connected.

Tapestry's built-in connectors automatically work with tons of your favorite sources - Tumblr, Mastodon, RSS, and more. But that’s only the beginning.

Third-party connectors can be added to Tapestry to allow it to work with even more sources. If it has a publicly-accessible feed on the internet, a connector can be built for it.
