Zoho's annual revenues surpassed $1B in 2021

Sridhar Vembu

  • Sridhar Vembu of Zoho said that no other company has built a billion dollar product company in India.
  • Zoho’s annual revenue in India increased by 77% in 2021 but contributed only 10% to the overall revenue figures.
  • The company also plans to open 100 network PoPs (point of presence) around the world in the next five years.

Chennai-based tech company Zoho Corp said that its annual revenues surpassed $1 billion in 2021, on Tuesday.
