Tilt Five – Bring holograms home – Play your favorite games in 3D

It’s time to bring your games to holographic life! A unique optical system that gives each player their own view into shared virtual worlds. No need for split screens or hiding card decks, players can still call out to each other and see the physical world around them.

The Tilt Five® system includes glasses that connect to a compute device you already own (like a Windows PC or Android phone) which is where your video games and apps already live! We support games where you currently buy them like Steam or GooglePlay. The Gameboard coated with a ‘retroreflector’ (millions of microscopic glass spheres) reflects the light from the T5 Glasses back to your eyes creating the tabletop hologram. You can direct, interact, and ‘touch’ the holograms using the wand controller and even your hands.
