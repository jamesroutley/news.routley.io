Dyalog: Escrow

Dyalog Ltd offers a fully verified escrow agreement with NCC Group so that, in the unlikely event that we become unable to continue to service our customers, our source code will be made available to those of our customers who subscribe to the service. The source code will be accompanied by a description of the software environment that is required to maintain Dyalog, including a copy of the full development environment.

This service is designed for clients who need to be able to guarantee to investors, customers or other stakeholders that software built upon Dyalog is maintainable in the event of default anywhere in your supply chain chain.

For more details, please see the joint brochure. To discuss subscribing to the Escrow arrangement, please contact Dyalog Ltd.
