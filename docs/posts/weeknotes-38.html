  • been a little while, sorry, hi.
  • i got told i'm using semicolons wrong in subediting and now i will never use them again. this is like dashes (not em dashes) all over again, it's almost like i learnt grammar through osmosis rather than being directly taught it! apparently you can use a semicolon to demarcate a list so i was doing that bit right, just not in clauses unless they could be two sentences. but don't quote me i didn't fact check them, grammar is for nerds.
