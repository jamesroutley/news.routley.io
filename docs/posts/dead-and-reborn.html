Dead and Reborn

I have abandoned my blog for a long time. This might be due to my sub par writing skills in English, a lack of time or—most likely—a mixture of both. I would like to change that, though, and I hope I will with this new format. It also means that I had to move from Ghost, which was never my thing to begin with, to a more DIY platform.

This blog solely consists of static pages that I write in markdown and then convert to html, not unlike Jekyll would do it. This tool, however, is written in zepto, my own concoction. The source is available on Github.

That's it for today! Have a pleasant start into 2017 and be sure to check back every once in a while.
