Ghostmoon.app – The Swiss Army Knife for your macOS menu bar

Gain access to system functionalities buried deep in System Settings or ones that require writing Terminal commands. Control the macOS User Interface. Keep the Mac awake during long tasks and quickly turn off displays when needed. Mass Eject External Drives in a safe way. Quickly switch Audio Output devices. Mute the Internal Microphone for privacy. Measure the speed and latency of your internet connection. Quickly restart the Network Stack. Reset internal macOS databases. Empty the Trash when it doesn't want to cooperate. Refresh the system without logging you out. Generate cryptographically Secure Passwords and Unique Identifiers. Battery Health. Essential System Stats. It's all there.

Microscopic app size & resource usage.

Apple Silicon and Intel processors.
