Start by creating your Kanban board from a template or from scratch. Add columns that match your project's workflow.

Optionally, configure sharing settings to allow collaboration with your team.

As incomes or expenses occur, add cards directly onto the appropriate column of your board.

You can easily drag and drop expense cards between columns, facilitating seamless organization and tracking of expenditures.

Budget Kanban automatically updates budget totals in real-time, providing with immediate insights into spending patterns and potential overruns.

Moreover, you can configure custom formulas to track specific metrics tailored to your project's requirements.

Drag-and-Drop Items

Move cards between columns to reflect changes in their budget allocation or progress, saving time and effort compared to manual data entry or editing.

Automatic Card Summation

Automating column summations not only prevents calculation errors but also keeps things flexible. This means you'll always have accurate financial data on hand, and the system can easily adapt to any changes you might need.

Real-Time Collaboration

Changes made by a member are immediately visible to others, ensuring that all team members have access to the latest financial data and can stay informed about budget adjustments or decisions.

Customizable workflows

You can modify columns as your financial needs evolve, accommodating changes in budgeting priorities, project requirements, or team dynamics without disrupting your workflow.

Security of financial data

Budget Kanban offer features designed to ensure the security and compliance of users' financial data, such as encryption, and access controls.
