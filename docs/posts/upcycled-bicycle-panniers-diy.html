Upcycled Bicycle Panniers DIY

Discarded plastic cans are an environmentally conscious and self-empowering way of carrying stuff, and that is important to us, but in the end we are really here for panniers that we can trust and enjoy using.

The Cannier design is based on ten years of experience, using these panniers both on long-distance trips through remote places and in daily urban setups.

Canniers and upcycling action in general is a statement that the person has grown to define the value of things around them on their own, and they are ready for action.

Explore the advantages, technical details, and our step-by-step how-to through the top menu. Embrace curiosity, connect with the material reality around you. It is a playful approach to life.
