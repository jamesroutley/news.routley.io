Join my new Risk Musings subscriber chat

Today I’m announcing a brand new addition to my Substack publication: the Risk Musings subscriber chat.

This is a conversation space in the Substack app that I set up for my subscribers — kind of like a group chat or live hangout. I’ll post short prompts, thoughts, and updates that come my way, and you can jump into the discussion.

Today at 1pm Eastern time, as announced in the newsletter, I’ll be on Chat to discuss two books I read recently: Human Compatible by Stuart Russell and HumanKind by Rutger Bregman. You can join now for a live chat or leave a comment later, and I’ll reply.

To join our chat, you can either download the Substack app or go to https://substack.com/chat while logged in to the Substack website on your computer, and you’ll see a row for my Risk Musings chat. Chats are not typically sent via email, so turn on push notifications so you don’t miss conversation as it happens.

Hope to see you there! Jump in to say hi, and if you have any issues, check out Substack’s FAQ.
