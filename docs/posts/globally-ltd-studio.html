globally.ltd/studio

As a part of releasing software and music for the last couple of years on my label globally.ltd, I’ve slowly built up a functioning recording studio in my basement and I’m excited to now open it to others.

gltd studio is a small, project-based art and recording studio in Glendale, Queens offering a variety of services including in-person (solo or supervised) sessions and remote mixing, mastering, and mentorship.

Rates will be pay-what-you-can, with a suggested range of $15-30/hr.

You can read more about it at: globally.ltd/studio/.
