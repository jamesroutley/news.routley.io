Crazy Calls Classics At the request of M&I Recording Studios, the Crazy Calls Classics recordings are no longer available in the Novelty Archives.

The purpose of the Novelty Archives is to preserve funny telephone answering machine recordings that are no longer in print for future generations to enjoy. However M&I Recording Studios is now in the process of bringing back Crazy Calls, therefore Crazy Calls can no longer be featured here on this page.

I encourage you to purchase a copy of the Crazy Calls from M&I Recording Studios and support the creative people at M&I who created these digital treasures we've enjoyed. http://www.mirecordingstudios.com/  M&I Recording Studios Website

The Novelty Archive is made up of hundreds of recordings from Cassettes, Compact Discs, and Archived MP3 Recordings. Some of the items found in the archive include:  Crazy Calls, Dr. Demento, CelebrityPhone, Celebrity AnswerAlls, Chick Hearn Telephone Answering Tape, You Rang?, Phonies, Hey! Wait for the Beep!, Radio Shack, Rich Little, At The Sound of The Beep, Pat Fleet Operator Recordings, Garfield, & Much More! Note: most of the recordings in the archive were archived from old cassette tapes; many of them are several decades old as a result, the sound quality will vary depending on the age and condition of the tape. 

If you have a CD, Cassette, or digital recording not archived, I'd love to hear them and add them to the archives!
