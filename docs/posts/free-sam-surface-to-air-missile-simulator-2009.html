F.A.Q.

About the simulator

What is this simulator about?

This is rather a system simulator, not a game.

The main goal during development was to simulate what the operators of the SAM battalion could see, and hear during engagement.

Technical problems

What is needed, to be able to start the simulator?

Computer must be able to display resolution 1280x1024, or above. Notebooks with smaller resolution, needs to be connected into external LCD to be able to play.

4Gb RAM is required to use the Karat camera daytime.

If you have less than 4Gb of RAM, or your 32bit OS doesn't allow SAMSIM to use at least 3Gb of it, you can see this error.

You can try these...

https://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/windows/desktop/aa366718(v=vs.85).aspx

... or switch the situation into "NIGHT".

Screen is corrupted, switches are in wrong position.

You need to set Windows screen magnification back to 100%.

3DAAR (3D After Action Report) tracks seem to crawl on surface.

Uncheck "Adjust altitudes to ground height " option in Google Earth, after you dropped your 3DAAR into it...

Game play

How can I score points?

No points are given during game play, as in reality.

When is a situation completed?

When you switch the system off. (Please check attached documentation)

Some situations are cannot be selected, why?

They are under construction, not ready yet.
