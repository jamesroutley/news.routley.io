Tell your crush you like them with zero-knowledge

How this works

You enter the name of your crush, we will hash it with the sha256 function and generate a unique link that you can share publicly. People can enter their names into this unique link to generate the hash of their name. If the generated hash matches, they'll be notified you are their crush, if not, they will be told they're not and no other info is revealed. Note we do not store any info, this app is entirely client side.
