No guarantees of future support but we will try hard to add support.

important for Framework 13 AMD laptop users

ROCm APU support is really important because Nvidia (Tegra / DIGITS), Intel (oneAPI) and Apple (MPS) all have supported their APUs. AMD is the only exception now.

I just went through the exercise of getting ROCm working again for my 780m Ryzen 9 7940HS APU, finishing about 10 minutes ago, to remind myself what it was like.

  1. Giving up on installing dependencies myself due to kernel version madness
  2. Downloading 15gb worth of rocm/pytorch docker container to try a version I knew used to work (6.1.3)
  3. Exporting HSA env hacks and compiling llama.cpp for use with the given gfx1102 version
  4. Realizing that despite this being the 'correct' version for my card, it didn't work, and having to downgrade to gfx1101 HSA
  5. This compile it worked ~50% of the time (50% of run attempts resulted in GPU hang)
  6. Deciding I wanted to try the latest and greatest
  7. Downloading 24gb worth of rocm/pytorch:latest (6.3.1 this time)
  8. Beating my head against a wall when gfx1101 now no longer works
  9. Stuffing my head back full of grey matter upon realizing that gfx1102 now does work
  10. Compiling and it again works, 50% of the time but hey, I get 10% faster token input speed over 6.1.3...

That experience is not conducive to doing anything hobby related, and I definitely would not expect that everyone's going to be knowledgeable enough or have enough patience to fiddle with their setups to find the working combinations. Please, I want to want your stuff :( . Even simple stuff like a pruned docker container would go a long way.

APU is currently the most valuable expansion platform. Consumer-grade dedicated GPUs are difficult to shake the dominant position held by NVIDIA cards; consumers do not trust AMD's GPUs can work as efficiently as CUDA in serious scenarios, making it hard for them to choose Amd GPU. With fewer people using Amd GPU, less feedback and optimization follow, creating a vicious cycle that makes ROCm worse and worse. In contrast, there are no such issues with APUs. Users will only think it's great that APUs can also be used for machine learning, leading to more adoption and better improvements over time. Because they are affordable, they can also be paired with large memory as VRAM, making laptops with APU the preferred choice for students to learn machine learning, helping to cultivate their user habits.
