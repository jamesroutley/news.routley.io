A Gripe about the Los Angeles Metro Bike App

WTF is up with the Los Angeles Metro Bike App? Have you ever tried it? Is the app bad or am I just dumb? I don't have answers, but lets explore.

The main flaw is renewing a *single* ride pass without a tap card. I would imagine it would work as follows:

  • buy a ride credit or multiple ride credits
  • view ride credit balance on your account

How much does Metro Bike cost?:

  • $1.75........1 Ride
  • $5...........24 hour pass
  • $17..........1 month pass
  • $150.........365 day pass

Let's buy some ride credit

So my beef is with taking 2 rides in a single day. You'd want to buy single rides (the $1.75 option) to spend the least amount of money. Ok - so lets buy some ride credits. Open the app and we will click on the account tab (circled in red):

Alright, let's renew or change a pass

Going great, lets click "RENEW PASS"

Ok this is where it gets a little silly. We have successfully renewed the pass

BUT the final screen in the process will ask you if you want to renew again???. The "My Account" section in green is meaningless. I bought two passes to confirm that it doesn't show multiple passes.

If you go back to your account tab none of the options will show you how many credits you have purchased. You have to contact Metro to check your balance.

Anyways, the app works and the bikes are great, but if anyone at Metro reads this please fix the user interface.
