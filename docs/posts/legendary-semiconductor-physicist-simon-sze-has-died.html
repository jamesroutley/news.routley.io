Simon M. Sze

Published by San Francisco Chronicle on Nov. 7, 2023.

Memories and Condolences

Get email updates for this page

Generations of scientists and engineers the world over in the area of semiconductors feel a connection to Simon Sze through the influence of his classic textbook "Physics of Semiconductor Devices" on their education.

May God bless you and your family in this time of sorrow.

A great pioneer whose books have shaped my career. Not only engineers, but society in general, has so much to thank him for his contributions to semiconductors.

Rest in peace Simon Sze, and thank you for the enduring legacy you leave behind. Your book helped me build my foundation for semiconductor devices, and I will forever be thankful to you.

One of the pioneers of solid-state technology and devices we owe so much to and who will never be forgotten. Thank you, Simon. Herman E. Maes

The torch of your influence will keep shining bright not only in the semiconductor world also in our lives, even in your absence. R.I.P. my teacher.
