“We are not cheating” (YH Kwon superconductor comments)

We Are Not Cheating YH Kwon goes rogue on Friday, showing up to the Metallic Multilayers Conference in Seoul unannounced. He presents the data from the to be retracted 3 author paper, affiliating himself with both the Qcenter and Korea University. Both institutions declare him persona non grata later, disavowing any affiliation since March of this year. The international audience of scientists is skeptical. Kwon speaks in Korean, with translator, annoying most of the Koreans there, who expect that someone of his stature speak English when presenting to foreigners. He declares he has a sample, and asks for some equipment so that he can demonstrate it. The equipment is not found, there is no demonstration of the Meissner effect. The international furore forces Lee to give an interview later in the day to Korean press. Flanked by TS Chair’s old buddy and 6th author, Hanyang University emeritus KH Aug, Lee retracts the 3 author paper, but declares the 6 author paper sound and in peer review. There will be corrections. It’s been submitted to APL Materials, an American Institute of Physics journal. Furthermore a group at US Dept of Energy run Argonne National Laboratories is racing to perform a verification in the coming weeks. Meanwhile simultaneously in Virginia, Hyun Tak is busy checking and correcting errors in the six author paper. He declares “We would not be cheating. It make mistake, because it was found, we will correct it”. The magnetic susceptibility issue identified turns out to be graph cut and paste error. JH Kim, also posts on social “We are not cheating” In total, all parties, each one experienced, and 3 of them eminent scientists, fully aware of the difference between diagmagnetism and superconductivity, while fighting with each other, are fully convinced that this is IT. Either it replicates, or we have an SBF style mind cult that seized control of some of the smartest scientists in Korea.
