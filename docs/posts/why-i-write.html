Why I Write

personal writing

"All that you touch You Change. All that you Change Changes you."

I have not written on this blog for many months now. Just couldn’t bring myself to do it. I could sit here and list off a bunch of excuses as to why, but I shan’t waste our time with that drivel.

Long story short, I was feeling lost and scared to put words on paper. I was unsure if anything I had to say was worth saying, worth reading, worth the churning of little internet packets to deliver it to you, dear reader. It’s been a heck of a time, but now I feel like I’m finally ready to start writing again. That quiet voice deep down started whispering and the urge to do more creative things welled up.

My focus will be to write more original, creative content, as I think it’s were I need to be right now. Tech-centric articles will continue to make their appearance here and creep in, if/when the fancy strikes me to write one, of course. I’m still a huge nerd after all! 😸 This is just one girl’s small gesture of defiance against the AI-produced writing and art that seems to encroach from all sides.

I want to help make art and beautiful things that other humans can appreciate and enjoy, to build my little island in the small-web archipelago.

Stay tuned folks! Be well and take care of each other.
