There was a time when cameras captured magic. Photos told stories of a certain moment in time, a reflection of reality, a physical artifact of lives.

Now, how we take, share, and create images has changed. Social media has made sharing images easy. Generative AI now creates any image we can imagine.

We've started to lose sight of what is real. We've lost our ability to find our bearings in an endless sea of copies and AI-generated noise.

By combining attested sensor data, zero-knowledge proofs, and a tamper-proof environment, we've built Roc Camera to capture verifiably real photos.

Camera Components:

  • • 4-inch IPS LCD Screen 720x720 with Capacitive Touch
  • • 16MP Sony IMX519 CMOS with 122° FOV lens
  • • Raspberry Pi 4 4GB RAM ARM Cortex-A72 1.5 Ghz
  • • LiPo 4000mAh Battery
  • • Uninterruptible Power Supply Board
