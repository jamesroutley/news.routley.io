Notes

On 18 April 2013, _why came back to publish a 95-page book (of sorts) by placing each page one at a time on a public print queue on whytheluckystiff.net throughout the day. The events of the day are captured in this IRC chat log. It's generally thought that this was _why's final act of closure, and so Steve Klabnik put all 95 pages together into a book called "CLOSURE".
