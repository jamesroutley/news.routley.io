Features and Benefits of Bluetooth Mesh 1.1 for Wireless Mesh Networking

Commercial and industrial applications like lighting require large-scale, low-power device networks where thousands of devices need to communicate with one another efficiently, reliably and securely. Commissioning and maintenance of such networks must also be easy and efficient in order to reduce the total cost of ownership.
