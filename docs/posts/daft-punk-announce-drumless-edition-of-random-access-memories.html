Daft Punk Announce Drumless Edition of Random Access Memories

Daft Punk have announced a new release in their 10th anniversary celebration of their final album, Random Access Memories. This one’s a fully drumless rendition of the album, which includes the just-released new version of the Chilly Gonzales collaboration “Within.” Listen to the percussion-free rendition of the track, and check out the new version’s artwork, below.

In May, the 10th anniversary edition reissue of the album arrived with a series of bonus tracks that included “The Writing of Fragments of Time,” a kind of behind-the-scenes look at the song’s creation with collaborator Todd Edwards. The duo has also shared a documentary series called “Memory Tapes” with collaborator interviews.

Daft Punk unexpectedly split up in 2021. Since then, Thomas Bangalter has shared his solo work Mythologies, while Guy de Homem-Christo collaborated with Travis Scott on Utopia.
