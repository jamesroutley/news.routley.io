Kirkland Roundabouts

How to Play

  • ← → Change lanes or turn
  • ↓ Space Brake
  • This game is designed to run on computers with a keyboard and at least a laptop-sized screen. There are on-screen controls for mobile devices but it's not optimal.
  • Reach your target exit — watch for other vehicles
  • Each lane change costs bonus points; collisions end the mission

About

Inspired by online discussions of the Kirkland roundabouts. Currently this game models the 85th St & I-405 interchange.

This game is satire. Not affiliated with the City of Kirkland or WSDOT. Do not use as driving instruction or navigation guidance.
