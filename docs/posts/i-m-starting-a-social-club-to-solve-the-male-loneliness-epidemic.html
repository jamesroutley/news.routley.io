How do I join Wave3 Social?

Start by attending one of our local mixers - open events where anyone can come meet current members and get a feel for the community. If the vibe is right and you're supported by existing members, you'll receive an invitation to join Wave3 as a full member with access to exclusive events and experiences.

What do members get access to?

Once you're invited into Wave3, you unlock access to curated events - everything from small-group hangouts and workshops to bigger socials. These are invite-only, thoughtfully planned, and focused on building real friendships and good times.

Is the club only for a specific type of guy?

Not at all. Wave3 is for any guy looking to build solid, meaningful friendships. Whether you're new in town, going through a life shift, or just tired of shallow hangouts - we're here for it. Our only ask is that you show up open and respectful.

How does the invite system work?

After attending a mixer, current members can vouch for new attendees they believe would be a great fit. If you're supported by enough members, you'll get an official invite to join the club. It helps us keep the vibe strong and the community aligned.

No stiff rules. Come as yourself. For mixers, think casual-cool - something you'd wear to meet friends for drinks. For private events, we'll share any dress suggestions in advance, but comfort and confidence always come first.

Can I bring a friend to events?

Absolutely. In fact, we encourage it. The more energy and openness in the room, the better. Just make sure they're aligned with what we're building - real connection, good vibes, and mutual respect.

Is there a cost to attend?

Mixers are usually free or low-cost to cover venue or food. Once you're a member, some events may have a small fee to help with planning, food, or space - but we keep it accessible and always let you know ahead of time.
