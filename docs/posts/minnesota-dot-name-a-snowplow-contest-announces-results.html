Minnesota DOT Name-a-Snowplow Contest Announces Results

MnDOT’s Name a Snowplow contest returns!

The Minnesota Department of Transportation is excited to announce that the agency’s Name a Snowplow contest returned for a second year! After 22,000 name ideas and 60,000 votes, it’s finally official: Plowy McPlowFace has eight new friends! One new name will be assigned to each of MnDOT’s eight districts later this month. The newly named trucks will join last years’ favorites in the fleet.

Top 50 finalists and top 8 winners

The top 8 winners

  • Betty Whiteout – District 8
  • Ctrl Salt Delete – District 7
  • The Big Leplowski – District 4
  • Plowasaurus Rex – Metro District
  • Scoop Dogg – District 3
  • Blizzard of Oz – District 2
  • No More Mr. Ice Guy – District 1
  • Edward Blizzardhands – District 6
