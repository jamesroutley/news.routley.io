Search giant to keep navigation apps separate, says no layoffs are planned

Updated Dec. 7, 2022 10:19 pm ET

Alphabet Inc.s Google plans to combine the team working on the mapping service Waze with the group overseeing the companys Maps product, as the search giant faces pressure to streamline operations and cut costs.

Google plans to merge Wazes more than 500 employees with the companys Geo organization, which oversees the Maps, Earth and Street View products, beginning on Friday, according to a Google spokeswoman.
