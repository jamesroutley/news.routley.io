‍The OneSignal Integration with Freshpaint

OneSignal currently uses a REST API that allows your engineering team to send event data from your site to OneSignal. Another way to say this: any time someone clicks on your site, you can send that information to OneSignal to trigger communication like a text, email, or push notification.

However, getting valuable data to OneSignal can be an engineering intensive exercise that often faces delays due to prioritization and engineering sprint timing. That’s where Freshpaint comes in.

Freshpaint autotracks actions on your site and sends the customer data to your marketing and analytics tools using prebuilt integrations. The result: product and marketing teams can get the data they need to OneSignal without waiting for engineering sprint cycles.

Because Freshpaint makes it easier for you to get data into OneSignal, you can craft smarter and more relevant messages. It’s all about the right message and the right time.

  • Instead of requiring your engineering team to code every customer event, Freshpaint uses Javascript to detect customer clicks and actions on your site automatically. Product and marketing teams can name and configure events using a visual editor, then send them to analytics tools like Mixpanel and advertising tools like Facebook using prebuilt integrations.
  • Ever get a great piece of customer insight only to spark a new question you can’t answer right away because you didn’t think to track it? Freshpaint gives you answers today instead of two weeks from now by backfilling the historical data from any new event and sending it to the tools in your tech stack.
  • For healthcare companies Freshpaint is HIPAA Compliant and gives you a purpose built toolset to help you manage PHI.
