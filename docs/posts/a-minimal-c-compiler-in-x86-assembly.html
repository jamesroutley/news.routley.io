The June Integration with Freshpaint

June can quickly spin up analytics, but it’s up to teams to get data from their website into their analytics, marketing, and sales software tools. Historically, June relied on Segment to support this function.

Today, we’re excited to announce that June customers can use Freshpaint to get data into June up to 14x faster.

At Freshpaint, we’re excited to offer a no-code alternative for powering tools like June. Freshpaint’s visual editor means anyone on the team can create and configure new events to share with June.

Freshpaint also works retroactively, so you can explore behaviors and create events that measure them even after your launches occur. When you create a new event in Freshpaint you’ll be able to backfill and send the historical data to June to get new insights right away.

Because Freshpaint makes it easier for you to get data into June, you’ll get everything you need to start growing your business without having to choose between using technical resources to build your product or help analyze your product.

  • Instead of requiring your engineering team to code every customer event, Freshpaint uses Javascript to detect customer clicks and actions on your site automatically.
  • Product and marketing teams can name and configure events using a visual editor, then send the data to June and other tools in your tech stack.
  • Ever get a great piece of customer insight only to spark a new question you can’t answer right away because you didn’t think to track it? Freshpaint gives you answers today by backfilling historical data from any new event and sending it to June.
  • For healthcare companies Freshpaint is HIPAA compliant thanks to a signed BAA and purpose-built features that help manage the flow of PHI to third party tools.
