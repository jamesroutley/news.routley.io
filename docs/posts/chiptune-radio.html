What Is This?

Chiptune Radio is a side project of Aleph Void, LLC — the music of sound chips past, streaming while you work, reverse engineer, or just vibe.

🎵

Pure Chiptune

Music composed for and inspired by classic sound hardware — the SID, the NES APU, the Game Boy's four channels, and the tracker scene that keeps them alive.

📡

Always On

A continuous radio-style broadcast on Twitch. Drop in any time, leave it on in the background, and let the playlist do the work.

👾
