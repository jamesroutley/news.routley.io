Kiwink® for Maintenance

By flashing, the LED can send an error code and the failure can be identified straight away in the diagnostic process. For some applications, the user could even diagnose himself the breakdown and help the customer service team bring the right replacement parts on site.

  • Reporting breakdown machine (Can help detect a persistent failure on a product at an earlier stage thanks to data recording)
  • Tools monitoring
  • Improved diagnosis
  • Optimization of the maintenance operations and procurement
  • Direct access web to repair instructions with the smartphone
  • Improved customer service
