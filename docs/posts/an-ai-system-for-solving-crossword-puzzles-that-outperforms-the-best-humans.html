An AI system for solving crossword puzzles that outperforms the best humans

Crossword puzzles test knowledge of trivia, commonsense, and wordplay, while also requiring one to reason across intersecting clues and answers. We built a dataset that contains millions of such clues and answers (available at https://huggingface.co/datasets/albertxu/CrosswordQA‚Ä¶). [2/8]

Our AI system works by first running a neural question answering model that produces top-K answers for each clue. It then uses probabilistic inference to generate a preliminary puzzle solution. Finally, we use a character-level language model to make local edits. [3/8]

These local edits make a huge difference: by proposing character flips and scoring them with the character-level ByT5, we improve our puzzle accuracy by more than 30%. Below we show a series of local edits. [4/8]

We evaluate our system on puzzles from various publishers and get great results! On New York Times puzzles, our system achieves 99.9% letter accuracy on themeless puzzles. We also can get over 80% of NYT puzzles correct without a single mistake. [5/8]

Finally, we competed in the 2021 ACPT, the world‚Äôs most prestigious human crossword tournament, and won first place out of over a thousand expert human competitors. [6/8]
