PPP Loan Map: See Where The Money Went

Ever wondered where all that PPP loan money ended up? Now you can see it—mapped out, visualized, and easy to explore.

What is this?

PPP Loan Map is a fun and interactive way to browse through public data on Paycheck Protection Program loans. Search by ZIP code, business type, or even how long a business has been around.

Why check it out?

  • ✅ See the big picture – Visualize loan distribution across the U.S.
  • ✅ Explore your neighborhood – Curious about local businesses? Find out who got funded.
  • ✅ No clutter, just data – Straightforward insights, no boring spreadsheets.
