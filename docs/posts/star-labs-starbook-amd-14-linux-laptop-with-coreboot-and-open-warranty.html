Uniquely progressive and progressively unique. Every detail of the StarBook is designed to be quietly configurable.

  • Extremely lightweight.
  • Extremely efficient.
  • Infinitely configurable.
  • Fewer blobs.

Closed-source

American Megatrends+EDKII

  • Industry Leading.
  • Secure Boot Support.
  • Password Protection.
  • Built-in User Interface.
  • EFI Capsule updates.

The StarBook supports either American Megatrends (AMI) Aptio V or coreboot. By default, the Intel ME is disabled on both coreboot and AMI. You can switch between AMI and coreboot firmware at any time.
