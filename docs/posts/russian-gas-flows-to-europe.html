Russian gas flows to Europe, sourced from ENTSO-G, gas storage levels from GIE.

Uzhgorod is on the Ukrainian border, and is also called Veľké Kapušany. The Yamal pipes are also called Mallnow, Jamal or Jagal. Nordstream meanwhile is also known as Greifswald in other tables.

As long as the EU storage withdrawal graph is positive, we are using up our reserves. Similar data but going back further in time and with prettier graphs & much more data can be found on this Bruegel page.

Data sourced daily from ENTSO-G, the European Network of Transmission System Operators for Gas and GIE, Gas Infrastructure Europe (thanks!). Very rough pricing indication based on Dutch TTF Gas May '22 (TGK22). Please let me know urgently if I am missing any pipelines, of if I am misintepreting the data!
