To you from the WWW

If you’re reading this, it means you’re accessing my blog from the world wide web. That’s cool and all, but have you heard of this thing called Gopher?

If I’ve piqued your interest, you might want to checkout the mirror of my blog at gopher.feyor.sh (gopher://gopher.feyor.sh). There’s not complete feature parity with the HTML version, but I’m quite happy with how it turned out.

Just a heads up that I use ANSI escape codes very liberally for code blocks and such, but not all Gopher clients like that (if you know me, I’m sure you can hazard a guess as to what Gopher client I’m using). Also, I assume some more basic clients will choke on CJK characters; all that is to say YMMV.
