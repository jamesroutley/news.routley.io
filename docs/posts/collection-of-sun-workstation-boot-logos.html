Collection of Sun Workstation boot logos

This repository contains Sun framebuffer logos, i.e., the logos you see when booting a SPARCstation, Ultra, or other Sun machine. Unless mentioned otherwise, these are pixel-exact copies of the logos taken straight from the firmware.
