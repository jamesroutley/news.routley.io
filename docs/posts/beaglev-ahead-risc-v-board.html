What is BeagleV®-Ahead?

BeagleV®-Ahead is an open-source RISC-V Single Board Computer (SBC) in the form factor of BeagleBone Black with compatible P8 & P9 cape header pins allowing you to stack your favorite BeagleBone cape on top to expand it’s capability. Featuring a powerful quad-core RISC-V processor, BeagleV®-Ahead is designed as an affordable RISC-V enabled pocket-size computer for anybody who want’s to dive deep into the new RISC-V ISA.

System on chip: Alibaba T-Head TH1520

  • 2GHz quad-core RISC-V 64GCV Xuantie C910 central processing unit (CPU)
  • 50GFLOPS, 3Mpixel/s Imagination BXM-4-64 graphics processing unit (GPU)
  • 4TOPS@INT8 neural processing unit (NPU) @ 1GHz
  • H.265/H.264 @ 4Kp75 video decoder
  • H.265/H.264 @ 4Kp40 video encoder
  • 2x image signal processor (ISP)
  • 2x digital signal processor (DSP)

Board Feature Highlights

  • BeagleBone-style form-factor
  • 4GB RAM and 16GB on-board eMMC flash
  • USB3 super-speed (5Gbps) micro-AB for power and data
  • Gigabit Ethernet
  • Integrated 2.4GHz and 5GHz WiFi
  • micro-HDMI, DSI, 2x CSI
  • Expansion via mikroBUS shuttle, BeagleBone cape headers
