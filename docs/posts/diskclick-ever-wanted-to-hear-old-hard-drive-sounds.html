DiskClick: Ever wanted to hear old hard drive sounds

Ever wanted to hear Old Hard drive sounds but don't want to go find a old pc or don't wanna get a old slow Clunker Hard drive. Well Fear not this program will let you emulate anything you want from a Floppy drive to a Rickroll. This Program uses your C drive to make noise based on real activity. If you want to load a new sample just click load folder and click from your own or ones i included. If you want to make your own just make a folder and put a load and looping wav in that folder. Looping is general ambiance/idle and load  is the emulated disk writing and reading data based on your actual C drive.
