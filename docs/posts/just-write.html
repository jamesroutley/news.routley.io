Just Write

I encourage you to start publishing your thoughts on a regular basis. Don’t overthink it. Start small. Just pick an existing platform and start writing short posts. If it helps, get drunk, choose a pseudonym, and post anonymously.

That’s what I’m trying to do with this short piece. I wasted so much time on overthinking and procrastinating that you could say this post was 10 years in the making.

Don’t over-engineer your site. Don’t even check the grammar. Just write 100 words and hit publish.
