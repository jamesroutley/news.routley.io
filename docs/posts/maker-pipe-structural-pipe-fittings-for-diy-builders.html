Our Connectors

Maker Pipe is a system of steel pipe connectors that turns ordinary EMT Conduit into an affordable building platform with endless possibilities. Designed so you can build easily with simple hand tools.

EMT Conduit

EMT Conduit is a galvanized steel pipe that is strong and trusted by builders. It's readily available in the electrical aisle of your local hardware or home improvement store.

easy

Building with conduit and Maker Pipe is easy because you don't need any special tools or skills. A 5mm hex wrench is all it takes to make a connection. Conduit can be cut with a simple hand held metal pipe cutter.

affordable

Cost shouldn't hold your DIY project back. That's why we designed our affordable connectors to pair with inexpensive electrical conduit. This means you get to save money while making your DIY dream a reality.

strong

Just because it's easy and affordable doesn't mean you have to compromise strength. Metal conduit and our Maker Pipe fittings are made of durable steel so you can build with confidence indoors and out.
