Rainbow Timeline

Today I got an email that my domain – rainbowtimeline.com – has not been renewed. I bought this domain years ago when I met up with a friend from the Family Constellation world at the Metropolitan Museum of Art. We came up with the name because of all the conversations in new age circles around time lines, for example: people are on different timelines, someone once told me he updates his timeline very quickly, timelines may diverge. My friend said she was on the rainbow timeline – I really dont know what this means – but I sort of wanted to live there too. I imagined the rainbowtimeline.org as perhaps a nexus of spiritual traditions, techniques, metaphysics, with lectures, classes and discussions on awakening and human development.

Conscious Coding

In the course of my life I became very interested in enlightenment and awakening. I consider this my purpose – and all our purposes – to awaken. And I believe that it is only through mass awakening that we can successfully address the crises of our time. In the course of this journey I have use many practices to liberate my consciouness.

Today, as I am writing this, I am reminded of my engagement in depth psychology – the psychoanalytic tradition pioneered by Freud and expanded by Jung, Lacan, and others. Freud says that one way the unconscious reveals itself in our idiosyncratic gestures and rituals, Jung says that the unconscious speaks in the images. and Lacan says that the unconscious speaks in language (he also uses terms from mathematics to systematized his theory of the unconscious.

Anyway I though- what if we thought of consciousness or human development as computation. What happens if the consciousness speaks in the language of computation.
