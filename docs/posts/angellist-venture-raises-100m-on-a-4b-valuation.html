AngelList Venture Raises $100M on a $4B Valuation

Two weeks ago, we closed a $100M round at a $4B pre-money valuation, led by Tiger Global with participation from Accomplice and others. 

We're also opening up a community round on AngelList itself for GPs who have made an investment on the platform in the last 12 months.

2021 was an incredible inflection point for AngelList Venture.

We launched RUVs, Stack, and hit $10B in assets supported. Almost 200 unicorns raised capital from GPs on AngelList, and 19 companies went public. In total, 800 GPs invested $3.6B from 38,000 LPs into 7,000 startups. We also sweated a lot of details.

We have a small team of 130 people. We will keep hiring with a very high bar and high leverage per employee.
