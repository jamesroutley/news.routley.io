And they had a boatload of production data to shape the specification. Meanwhile, we have only had a Big Buck Bunny demo, and I believe the standard has veered off course as a result.

Okay they didn‚Äôt say that, but this is exactly the mentality that MoQ needs right now. Just build something. Just release something. Just do it.
