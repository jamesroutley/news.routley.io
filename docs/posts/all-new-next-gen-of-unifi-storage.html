All-New Next Gen of UniFi Storage

Storage has long been the backbone of modern networking, and UniFi is raising the bar once again. The latest UNAS lineup combines thoughtful design with cutting-edge performance, ensuring that whether you are a home user, a growing business, or an enterprise, there is a model tailored to your needs.

UNAS 2: Compact and Elegant Storage

The UNAS 2 is designed for simplicity without sacrificing innovation. With a clean, cable-free look and versatile features, it brings storage management down to a single, streamlined device.

  • PoE-powered single-cable setup
  • Multi-bay storage design
  • USB-C external device support
  • Responsive LCM screen for quick navigation

The UNAS 2 proves that simplicity and performance can coexist in a desktop NAS.

UNAS 4: Scalable Storage Power

For those who need more capacity, the UNAS 4 takes the foundation of the UNAS 2 and doubles its potential. It offers not only expanded storage but also the speed of NVMe SSD technology.

  • Support for additional HDDs
  • Integrated M.2 NVMe SSD slots
  • Optimized for high-performance storage
  • Same streamlined setup as the UNAS 2

The UNAS 4 is a natural step up, balancing storage growth with optimized high-performance storage.

UNAS Pro 8 and UNAS Pro 4: Professional-Grade Storage

The UNAS Pro series is engineered for environments where performance and reliability cannot be compromised. The UNAS Pro 8 is a powerhouse designed for enterprise-scale workloads, while the UNAS Pro 4 delivers similar capabilities in a compact 1U chassis for rackmount installations. Both models provide flexibility and efficiency tailored to professional deployments.

  • Multiple 10G interfaces with MC-LAG support
  • Dual M.2 NVMe SSD bays for lightning-fast caching
  • Modular, hot-swappable power supplies (UNAS Pro 8)
  • Space-saving 1U rackmount design (UNAS Pro 4)
  • Enterprise-grade reliability across the Pro lineup

The UNAS Pro 8 and UNAS Pro 4 deliver the speed, scalability, and resilience professionals depend on.

UniFi Drive: The Platform Behind It All

All UNAS models run on UniFi Drive, a license-free storage management platform that evolves quickly with user needs. It enhances productivity with integrations and customization that make it stand out in the market.

  • AWS S3, Backblaze B2, and Wasabi backup integrations
  • Active Directory support for SMB sharing and access controls
  • Customizable fan control with Quiet, Balanced, and Cooling modes
  • No recurring license costs

UniFi Drive turns every UNAS system into a powerful, scalable storage platform.

The new UniFi storage lineup delivers practical innovation across every level of use.
