London ISP CommunityFibre Discount 3Gbps FTTP Plan to £50

London-focused UK ISP CommunityFibre (CF), which has already covered 500,000 homes with their Fibre-to-the-Premises (FTTP) broadband network and aims to reach 2.2 million homes in the city by the end of 2024, has discounted the price of their top symmetric speed 3Gbps plan to just £50 per month for 24-months (normally £99).

The new deal will be available to order from now until 29th June 2022, although the special offer on their top 3Gbps tier – one of the fastest broadband packages in the UK – is only available for the first 150 subscribers to take it. Alternatively, they’ve also discounted their 500Mbps plan to just £27 per month for 24-months (£42.50 thereafter).

New CommunityFibre customers can expect to receive a free installation, symmetric speeds and unlimited usage. The 3Gbps package also gives you a high-end WiFi 6 router, which also features a 10Gbps LAN port and that’s not something we often see with consumer-focused broadband packages.
