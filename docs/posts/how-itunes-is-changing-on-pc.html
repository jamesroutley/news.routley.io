  • Copyright

When iTunes was originally released, it had everything you needed to be entertained. Now, there are individual apps for all your media:

  • Apple Music : Find the songs, albums, and artists you love. Get unlimited access to millions of songs and your entire music library, on all your devices.

  • Apple TV : See all your favorite TV, all in one app, including exclusive Apple Originals from Apple TV+, popular movies to rent or buy, and more.

  • Apple Devices : Sync the contents of your media library to your Apple devices from your Windows PC.

  • iTunes : iTunes remains the home for your audiobooks and free podcasts.

When you install the Apple Music or Apple TV app, you can only use those apps for your music, TV shows, and movies; you can continue to use iTunes to listen to your audiobooks and podcasts.

If you don’t install Apple Music or Apple TV, you can continue to use iTunes for all your media. These are the basics:
