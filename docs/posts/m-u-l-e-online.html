M.U.L.E. Online

Classic M.U.L.E. from the early 80s for modern computers!

Features / Support:

  • Local multiplayer
  • Online multiplayer (up to 3 local players)
  • Gamepad control
  • Keyboard control
  • Mouse control
  • Windows (10 or later)
  • Mac (Sierra 10.12 or later)
  • Linux (glibc 2.27+)
