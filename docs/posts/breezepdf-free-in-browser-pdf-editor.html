Privacy First

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is BreezePDF free?

Yes! The web editor is completely free with 3 downloads per month. If you need unlimited downloads, the Desktop Pro subscription gives you a native macOS app with no limits.

How is my privacy protected?

Your files never leave your device. All processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. We don't upload, store, or have any access to your documents.

Do I need to install anything?

The web version works in any modern browser with no installation. The desktop version is a native macOS app that you download and install like any other application.

What formats can I export to?

You can save your work as PDF, export to DOCX (Word), or extract data to CSV for spreadsheets.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Absolutely. Cancel anytime from your account. Your subscription stays active until the end of the current billing period — no surprise charges.

What happens after 3 free downloads?

You can still open and edit PDFs without limits. Downloading requires either a Desktop Pro subscription or waiting until your free downloads reset next month.
