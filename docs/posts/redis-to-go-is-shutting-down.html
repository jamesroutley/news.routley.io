Redis To Go is shutting down

On August 8th, 2022 , RedisToGo will be shutting down its managed Redis service. In order to avoid any service interruptions, we recommend beginning a migration plan as soon as possible.

HOW DOES THIS IMPACT ME?

After August 8th, 2022 , RedisToGo instances will be shut off. To avoid application disruption, you will need to migrate your data to another Redis-as-a-Service provider.

WHAT OPTIONS ARE AVAILABLE?

With any deprecation of service, we recognize the need to provide other options as viable replacements. Our support team is prepared to help you with the following options:

ObjectRocket for Redis offering. You would receive the same level of service on your database instance(s), but the instance(s) would need to reside in one of these locations: N. Virginia (IAD), Dallas (DFW), Chicago (ORD), London (LON)

Please note, ObjectRocket Redis DOES NOT have a free tier plan available. More information about the ObjectRocket Redis offering can be found on our website at: https://www.objectrocket.com/managed-redis/

If you decide to move to another Redis-as-a-Service provider, you can download a backup of your instance(s) by following the steps listed via this link: https://redistogo.com/documentation/backup
