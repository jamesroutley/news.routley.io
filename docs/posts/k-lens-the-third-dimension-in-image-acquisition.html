K|Lens – The third dimension in image acquisition

THE WORLD'S FIRST

LIGHT FIELD LENS

  • A revolutionary light field lens for capturing depth information and “rich light field data” for all full-frame cameras
  • Works in photo and video modes
  • Includes free dedicated software that calculates depth information and makes it available for all your post-processing needs
  • Developed and produced in cooperation with Carl Zeiss Jena GmbH, Germany

Learn more

SHOOT 2D, GET 3D

The depth in space of every single pixel in every K|Lens image or video frame is calculated for you. And the resulting depth information is given to you as depth maps, depth channels and/or point clouds, as well as your standard RGB image or video.

﻿

A NEW WORLD OF POSSIBILITIES

The photos and videos you create with the K|Lens are a perfect match for all 3D displays, be it stereo, holographic, or light field.

But that’s not all: because depth information and “rich light field data” also open up new worlds of creativity to you in post-processing!

Focus pulls, re-focusing, blur modification and simplified segmentation are just the beginning. And, imagine this: you can also apply ALL of the filters and tools in a depth-based way. And, on top of that, you always have access to multiple perspectives of your same scene to play with. 

Perhaps now you can start to imagine this new plethora of possibilities!

RICH LIGHT FIELD DATA

In our in-house developed “SeeDeep” software you can generate rich data for each and every one of your photos:

  • The original “kaleido image” in JPG and RAW formats
  • Nine individual images with slightly different perspectives as JPGs
  • The center image, which, thanks to “Multiview Super-resolution” consists of around 50% of the sensor’s resolution
  • Depth map for the center image
  • A point cloud of the recorded scene

And in video mode, a depth channel is available in addition to your full-resolution video!
