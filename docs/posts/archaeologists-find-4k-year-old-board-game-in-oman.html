Archaeologists find 4,000-year-old board game in Oman

A rare 4,000-year-old board game made of stone has been uncovered by archaeologists in Oman. Marked with ‘playing fields and cup holes’ the discovery was made during excavations of settlements near the village of Ayn Bani Saidah in the Qumayrah Valley dating back to the Bronze and Iron Ages.
