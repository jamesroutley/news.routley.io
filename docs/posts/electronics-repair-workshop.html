Electronics Repair Workshop

FUTO is excited to start offering free open to the public repair workshops in Austin, TX for anybody who wants to gain familiarity with a microscope, soldering station, and electronics troubleshooting equipment; run by the world famous Rossmann Repair Group. Do not ask to sign up, you show up with something to work on and that is that!

We eventually plan to have these available every day, once Louis’ staff has completed their full move to Texas. For the time being, check back this page regularly for availability, as the schedule is subject to change!
