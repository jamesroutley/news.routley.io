Microsoft will recognize Game Workers United, the union formed from QA staffers from Raven Software, when the tech giant acquired Activision Blizzard later next year. In a roundtable, Xbox boss Phil Spencer told the Xbox Game Studios staff that he supported unions and will work with Game Workers United.
