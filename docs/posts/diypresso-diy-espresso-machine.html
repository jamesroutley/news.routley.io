Are you ready to build your own espresso machine and be part of the new coffee making experience?

In October 2022, Bernard and Pieter set out to revolutionize the espresso experience with diyPresso. Their goal was ambitious: a DIY espresso machine offering both assembly ease and consistent, fully adjustable extraction quality.

Their solution was ingenious yet simple. Armed only with a screwdriver and a wrench, users could effortlessly assemble the diyPresso, thanks to its streamlined design. To ensure optimal extraction, the duo integrated advanced sensors measuring key parameters like temperature, time, and weight. This allowed users unprecedented control over the brewing process.

By the summer of 2023, their first prototype emerged, followed by extensive testing with a select group of 15 beta-testers. Incorporating invaluable feedback, Bernard and Pieter refined the diyPresso, fine-tuning its performance to perfection.

Now, after months of dedication and refinement, the diyPresso one is ready to redefine home espresso brewing. With its user-friendly assembly and precise extraction control, it promises an unparalleled coffee experience for enthusiasts worldwide.
