Graph paper: questions & answers

What is graph paper?

Graph paper — also called grid paper or squared paper — is paper printed with an even grid of squares that helps you draw to scale, plot data and keep work neat. FreeGraphPaper turns any grid into a clean, print-ready PDF.

Is the printable graph paper free?

Yes. Every graph paper PDF here is completely free — no login, no sign-up and no watermark. Download as many sheets as you like.

How do I print graph paper at the right size?

Download the PDF and print at 100% scale (turn off “fit to page”) so a 5 mm or 1/4 inch grid measures exactly right on paper.

What graph paper sizes and spacings can I download?

Popular options include 5 mm, 1 cm, 1/8 inch and 1/4 inch graph paper on A4, US Letter and the full ISO A/B/C range — plus dot grid, isometric, hexagon and lined paper.
