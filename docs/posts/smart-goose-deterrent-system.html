Smart Goose Deterrent System

Problem

For years, geese have been leaving their mark on our docks and in our yards. People have tried a number of "solutions" to get rid of them: fences, reflective tape, plastic animals, dogs, kites, blinking lights, loud noises, ... Unfortunately, none have done the job. Geese figure out each system's weakness and eventually have their way.

Solution

To outsmart geese, we built a system that uses motion detection, Image Recognition, and AI to detect geese and people. If it finds geese, it activates sprinklers. If it finds people or nothing of interest, it disables sprinklers. We call it the Smart Goose Deterrent System. (see how SGDS works)
