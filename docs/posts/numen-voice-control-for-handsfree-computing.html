Numen: Voice Control for Handsfree Computing

I'm excited to share the voice control I've been using and working on for the last two years. I was looking for something simple and open with accurate offline speech recognition, and thought it would be worth making myself if it could help other people too.

If it's not on your distro yet, you can find the source here. I've also made a desktop environment that works well with voice control, called tiles.

You can email ~geb/public-inbox@lists.sr.ht if you need any help, or join our Matrix chat at #numen:matrix.org.
