“Teds” Age: 15-30 years Fanatics of 50s Rock n’ Roll. Clothing is 50s style: Skinny jeans, winklepicker shoes. Hair in style of R&R-times (Elvis quiffe, “Duck”). Politically uninterested. Hardly noticed operationally, active mostly at birth/death days of idolized rock stars.

“Tramps (bums)” Age: 20-30 years Classic manifestation of the negative-decadent youth in the 70s. Blues fans. Long hair, jean parka, Birkenstock (literally: “Jesus slippers”), beards, sometimes fashion accessories similar to Hippies. Not bound to any political worldview.

“Skins” Age: 15-22 years Society-threatening youths as lone wolves or in loose groups. Baldness/short hair, bomber jacket, skinny jeans, high steel-toe boots studded with nails and iron. Hostile political attitude, glorification/use of brutal violence (hooliganism).

“Heavies” Age: 15-25 years Fans of the so-called Heavy-Metal music (extremely hard rock). Black leather clothing, leather cap, rivet-studded jackets and pants, wearing heavy chains, etc. normal to semi-long hair. Aggressive attitude, partly neo-fascist tendencies.

“Goths” Age: 15-20 years Glorification of creepy effects, satanic and death cult, fans of the group “The Cure.” Black dyed hair, sticking out in all directions. White powdered face, black clothes. Wearing of crucifixes upside down. Totally uninterested in politics and society.

“Punks” Age: 15-22 years English: Garbage/trash. Filthy, torn, paint-sprinkled clothing, utensils like safety pins/razor blades, multi-coloured, scraggy hair, Mohawk (“Iroquois” haircut). Hostile political attitude, rejection state and societal norms. Glorification of anarchy.

“New Romantics” Age: 15-18 years Dropout/slacker movement. Black/grey clothes, red/black contrasts as a symbol of anarchy. Hair dyed black, short but longer in front, falling into face, obscuring the eyes. Same hostile positions as punks but with full rejection of violence.

“Poppers” Age: 13-20 years Appearance as breakdancers. Exclusively interested in disco and dance. Adolescent with extremely modern clothes. Hairstyle similar to New Romantics, multi-colored. Completely uninterested in politics. Mostly involved in fights with the Heavies.
