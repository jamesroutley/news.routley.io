Boomloom: Think with your hands

Our loom is designed for the way people naturally learn to create: by doing.

That bar at the top separates the warp threads for you as you turn the knob. So plain weave, such as tapestry, is a breeze.

But there’s more — the bars also make patterns like a 4-shaft loom. Without multiple technical steps, pressing levers, or deciphering drafts. Instead it’s direct and intuitive: just turn the bar between rows to build the pattern. Each of the 5 bars creates a different weave structure with endless variations, so even beginners can explore complex designs immediately.
