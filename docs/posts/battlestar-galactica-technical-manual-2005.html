Battlestar Galactica: Technical Manual (2005)

Welcome to the "Battlestar Galactica: Tech-Manual." Due to the many inconsistencies found in the realm of "Battlestar Galactica" science, we have dedicated our efforts to applying real science to the popular 1979 TV series created by Glenn A. Larson.

Scientific inaccuracies plagued many of the science fiction movies/series during the late 70's and early 80's. It was not until the mid-80's that Hollywood began to really tap NASA and other aerospace scientists for assistance in fine tuning the technobable being used in science fiction films and TV shows.

Listed on this site are the rational explanations as to how the science and technology of "Battlestar Galactica" would have worked in the real world. I hope you enjoy the site and, if you have any problems or questions, please feel free to contact me at: galactica@tecr.com.
