Will Hare replace C? Or Rust? Or Zig? Or anything else?

Will Hare replace C? Or Rust? Or Zig? Or anything else? May 2, 2022 by Drew DeVault

Nope.

If the short answer leaves you wanting, keep reading. I must admit that this sentence from the Hare announcement may have caused some confusion:

Hare is not interested in taking over the world, and I do not recommend that programmers start rewriting their C programs in Hare.
