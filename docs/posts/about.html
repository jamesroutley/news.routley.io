About

Hi, I’m Elijah!

📸 by Jose Oliva Martinez @thatonejackedmexican

I’m an artist, a programmer, and an enthusiast of running around in the woods and climbing things. I am from Arlington Virginia, but have spent the last few years in Memphis, TN, and recently relocated to NYC. Ask me about Peace Corps, converting a van into a home, or Jenny Odell’s excellent book “How to Do Nothing”.

I created this blog as a low-pressure way to chronicle my ideas, art, and experiences. The added bonus is that it’s also a way for me to share them! If you have any questions, feel free to reach out at elijahclimbs at gmail dot com.
