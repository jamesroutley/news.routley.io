SCRAP METAL RECYCLING - INDUSTRIAL SURPLUS - PROPS

Since 1958, Apex has been at the forefront of non-ferrous scrap metal recycling and liquidation in Los Angeles County. Along the way, Apex has found sustainable and environmentally friendly solutions in prolonging the life of recyclable goods. We assist both businesses and individuals with their everyday needs. Specializing primarily in aviation surplus and consumer industrial, Apex is pleased to offer thousands of products at an affordable price. In addition to surplus, Apex is also an industry leader in set decor and prop rentals. Through 60 years of accumulation, we are home to some of the most unique and hard-to-find items in the world. Our yard stretches over an acre and our 10,000 square foot store is filled with new inventory daily.
