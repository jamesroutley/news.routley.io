How it works

Under 30 seconds a day. No feed, no doomscrolling — just intention.

01

Import your people

Pick the 5–10 people who matter most. Not your whole address book — just the ones you'd hate to lose touch with.

02

Set your rhythm

Choose a frequency for each person and when you'd like to be nudged. Weekly for Mom, monthly for college friends — you decide.

03

Get nudged, check in

A gentle notification arrives. Reach out, then log the interaction in seconds. Close the app and get back to your day.

04

Watch your garden bloom

Your relationships flourish visually as you stay connected. An 80-year Harvard study suggests close relationships are a top predictor of long-term happiness.
