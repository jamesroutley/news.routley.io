Juxtaposition: When Action is a Negative, or Inaction is a Positive.

A juxtaposition:

Two items thus juxtaposed can complement each-other, like a pair of writers from different centuries who found the same conclusion—but with wording or reasoning that differs wildly! Or they may mostly contrast each-other—until you stare at them for a time.

I -love- weird, ironic, or amusing juxtapositions.

“All of humanity’s problems stem from man’s inability to sit quietly in his own room.”

“They also serve who only stand and wait.”

—Milton, “When I Consider How My Light Is Spent”
