Mortal Kombat+

Klassic Mortal Kombat Updated

Midway released three classic arcade fighting games known as Mortal Kombat, Mortal Kombat II and Ultimate Mortal Kombat 3.

We spent months disassembling the original Midway code and fixed known issues that were never fixed by Midway without harming the original gameplay. We also added some new exciting features as well as some new exciting secrets to be found.

The result is the classic Mortal Kombat games we know and love made by the original Midway team in the nineties simply… updated.

Want to play it? Here’s how

If you want to get your hands on the new ROM’s and start doing some fatalaties, there are a few ways to go about doing it

MAME +

Our custom build of the Multiple Arcade Machine Emulator is the second best way to play the ROM’s. This is a software emulator which will run the game on almost basically any PC hardware.

Fightcade

Similar to MAME, but instead runs a custom emulator in an Electron based client called FBNeo. By using Fightcade, you not only can play your game under emulation, but you can also play online. Yes, you can play with anyone from around the world and what’s even better is Fightcade support GGPO technology which assists in reducing lag effects.

In a Store

There are multiple arcade bars around the United States of America who run Mortal Kombat Plus ROM’s on their arcade machines. Simply check out this list of vendors available and feel free to give their store a visit to play (it is reccomended to call in advance to ensure the Mortal Kombat Plus version you want to play is up and running).
