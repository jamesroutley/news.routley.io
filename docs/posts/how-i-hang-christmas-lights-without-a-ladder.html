How I hang Christmas lights without a ladder

20lb magnets and triangle eye nuts via Amazon. Nylon spacer needed b/c the hole in the triangle nut is big.

Carefully splitting the cord on the lights and adding the magnet, bolt, and nylon spacer.

Everything assembled, and nut painted white to match the house trim.

Magnet attaches to the roof edge flashing which is metal.

Each magnet is put into place with this pole and yellow hook attachment. I modified the hook slightly to better catch the triangle handle.

Magnets placed every 4’.

Done! The trickiest part of everything is at the beginning. A single magnet placed at the peak of the roof can’t support the weight of all the Christmas lights on its own. I would recommend putting hook at that spot if you don't have something there already (I had a wood bracket).
