Why not just use an Amazon Alexa, Google Home or a Bluetooth speaker to cover my bathroom noise?

By manually starting music you are indicating you are about to do something embarrassing. Loodio always plays music automatically no matter your intentions of the visit. 

Loodio also blocks the sound extra well due to a soft waterfall sound that plays simultaneously with the music. 

Does it have a microphone?

No.

Does it run on batteries?

Yes!

Does it have Bluetooth?

Yes!
Loodio has bluetooth and can be used as a bluetooth speaker.

Will it disturb my sleeping partner if I use the bathroom at night?

No.

Loodio has a night mode which means it will be quiet during nightly visits. Can be configured to follow your schedule.

What is the technology behind Loodio?

Loodio uses a motion detector to sense when someone enters. It then plays music and sound cancelling sounds as long as it detects someone in the room.
