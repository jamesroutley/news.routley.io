getting weird errors in egglog Python... and I am realizing they are from things like this:

    def append(self, i: IntLike) -> TupleInt:
        return TupleInt(self.length() + 1, lambda j: Int.if_(j == self.length(), i, self[j]))

This looks fine, but it's not! Because let's say we are append to an empty list and then we index into it. The self[j] part will eval with the empty list, even though its on the false path...
