Show HN: A better way to timeblock your day

Carve out focus time amongst the meetings

Timeblocking is a super-powerful productivity tool where you divide your day into chunks of time where you plan to complete specific tasks.

If you're a seasoned timeblocker, are you having to manage both your calendar and to-do list to achieve productivity bliss? Well, not anymore. Taskable makes it super quick and easy with our Timeblock feature. Simply plan the tasks you want to do each day, and then drag and drop them into your calendar. Not only will the blocked time appear in Taskable, but it will reflect in your calendar as well, that way no one can schedule a meeting in your focus time.
