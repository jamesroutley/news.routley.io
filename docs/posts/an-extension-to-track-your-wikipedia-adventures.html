Show HN: An extension to track your Wikipedia adventures

Visualizes your daily Wikipedia adventures

Wiki Journey tracks Wikipedia articles you visit and visualizes them on a tree where you can see which articles led to which. Each day a new tree is created, you can also view your older trees. You can share your trees as well!
