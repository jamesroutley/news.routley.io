Virtually unlimited scale

Scales to meet any workload demand without database sharding or instance upgrades. It provides virtually unlimited horizontal scaling with the flexibility to independently scale reads, writes, storage, and compute.

Remove infrastructure management as there are no servers to provision, patch, manage, or upgrade. Aurora DSQL automatically handles updates without downtime and with zero impact on application performance.
