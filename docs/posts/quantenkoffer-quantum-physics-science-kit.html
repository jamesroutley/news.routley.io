Quantenkoffer: Quantum Physics Science Kit

Quantum physics science kit

The Quantenkoffer is a quantum physics science kit for a multitude of experiments from 100 years of quantum physics. Perform your experiments with a wide variety of optical tokens or create exciting setups yourself. Experience and grasp quantum physics!

Hands-on quantum physics

The key feature is its flexibility with regard to generation and detection of visible laser light, single photons and even entangled pairs. Its optical elements, mechanical components and digital circuits are integrated in order to cover a range of experiments and topics of quantum physics as wide as possible.

Robust and safe

The sensitive optics and sensors are mounted safely and shockproof in the interior of the robust case. The integration of the powerful laser source below the playing surface enables a safe handling of the laser radiation.
