FAQ

  • Who is Candix for?

    Candix is open to anyone but works best for people in the software industry who reside in the US or Europe.

  • I don't want to switch jobs right now. Should I still create a Candix profile?

    Yes, that's actually the best timing. You have the option to mark your profile as inactive, which keeps it discoverable but indicates that you're not open to receiving opportunities. Interested companies can place an alert on your profile and be notified on the day you start seeking opportunities, boosting your future search.

  • How quickly can I receive a job opportunity on Candix?

    It's hard to say, as it it depends on many factors: the industry you're in, your location, your level of experience, etc. We've seen it take a couple of hours for some and a couple of months for others.

  • Can my current employer come across my Candix profile?

    No. Your profile is hidden from both current and previous employers, as well as from any company you add to your blacklist. Additionally, we enforce strict confidentiality measures to keep your data secure, including requiring companies to sign an NDA before they can access the pool and enforcing a single point of access per company.

  • Is Candix truly free?

    Yes, Candix is completely free, forever.

  • How does Candix make money?

    Candix charges recruiters for access to its talent pool.

  • Is there a selection process?

    No, there is no selection process, but there is a review process to ensure that your profile is legitimate. This process is almost instantaneous if you signed up with LinkedIn, but it can take a bit longer otherwise.
