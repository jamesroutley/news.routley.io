Google Pixel 9 Pro

How do I choose between Pixel 9 Pro and Pixel 9 Pro XL?

For the first time ever, a Pixel pro phone comes in two sizes – so you can get the pro experience in the form that best suits your lifestyle.

If you prefer a larger display, then the 6.8" Pixel 9 Pro XL is a great choice. If you have previously purchased a pro phone and like that size, Pixel 9 Pro XL is more comparable.

If you prefer a smaller display, then the 6.3" Pixel 9 Pro is a great option, and the newest size we offer in this model. Learn more about size.

What is an unlocked Pixel 9 Pro?

An unlocked Pixel 9 Pro is a phone that isn’t tied to a specific carrier. When you purchase an unlocked Pixel 9 Pro, you get to choose which carrier or plan works best for you. Most phones in the Google Store come unlocked.

To use a SIM-unlocked phone:

Is Pixel 9 Pro dual SIM?

Pixel 9 Pro has dual SIM functionality. You can use two SIMs: one physical SIM card and one eSIM. You can choose which SIM to use for which action, like messaging or calling. This option is called Dual SIM Dual Standby (DSDS). On Pixel 9 Pro if your carrier allows, you can use two eSim profiles simultaneously and connect to two 5G networks in dual SIM mode at the same time.

What is the difference between connecting my Pixel 9 Pro now with a carrier and connecting it later?

The Google Store offers carrier activation with AT&T and Verizon. If you activate with a carrier now, we’ll connect your Pixel 9 Pro to your account.

AT&T customers: Once you receive your new Pixel 9 Pro, call AT&T to complete your activation. Your Pixel will arrive ready to go with your number and plan information. If you connect with a carrier later, once your new Pixel arrives, you can insert a physical SIM card or use eSIM from any carrier that provides service for Pixel 9 Pro.

Does Pixel 9 Pro have 5G network compatibility?
Yes, Pixel 9 Pro works with 5G networks. The type of 5G service depends on both the phone model and the carrier network. Learn more about 5G.
What is included in the Pixel 9 Pro box?
Your Pixel 9 Pro purchase comes with a 1 m USB-C® to USB-C® cable (USB® 2.0) and SIM tool. Pixel 9 Pro does not come with a 45W USB-C Power Charger, but that can be purchased separately.
How does the Google Store trade-in program work?
You can trade in Google Pixel phones and phones from most other manufacturers. How much you will get for your trade-in depends on the phone, model, manufacturer, and condition. During your purchase of a new Pixel phone, answer a few questions accurately, and once we receive the phone within the specified time frame and verify its condition, you’ll most likely receive the full amount of the estimated refund. Keep in mind that our trade-in partner needs to receive your phone within 30 days of receiving your new phone, and the condition needs to match what you told us. Learn more about Google Store trade-in.
What is Preferred Care?
Preferred Care is worry-free protection for your device, which provides mechanical breakdown coverage (after the one-year manufacturer’s warranty) and covers accidental damage (including drops, liquid resistance failures, and cracks). You can also get access to participating walk-in centers for screen repairs and unlimited access to specially trained agents whenever you call. Available 24/7. Best of all, you can pay for the coverage in a single payment or via monthly installments. Learn more about Preferred Care.
How do I switch from an Android phone or an iPhone to Pixel 9 Pro?
Switching to Pixel is easy. You can transfer your stuff, like photos or messages, from your old phone to your Pixel in a few simple steps. Learn more about switching to Pixel.
Where can I find setup instructions and tips for my new Pixel 9 Pro?
Move your stuff over, personalize your settings, and explore Pixel features to get the most out of your phone. Get more help 24/7 at g.co/pixel/support.
