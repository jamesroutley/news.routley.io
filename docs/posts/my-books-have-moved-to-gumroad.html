My books have moved to Gumroad

Just some random administrivia: quite some time back, I put together collections of posts and released them as two different ebooks: The Bozo Loop (2012) and The Stupid Hour (2013).

They were initially put on Amazon, and also showed up in some other strange places for a while - Barnes and Noble, and briefly, right here on my own home-grown "store" that used Stripe for billing.

As of earlier this week, they are no longer on Amazon at my direction. Instead, I put them on Gumroad as .epub files. They are still DRM-free, so once you have a copy, you can shove them onto all of your devices without having something "phone home" to approve it first.

If you wanted a copy but didn't want to deal with Amazon or the Kindle ecosystem, hey, now's your chance. If this works out, I might do some other things this way, too.
