Show HN: I made a Note-Taking app for people who keep texting themselves

Take notes as if you're chatting

Strflow is a note-taking app designed for intuitive and seamless jotting.

Every note you type accumulates in chronological order with its timestamp. Built on this straightforward mechanism, we offer a refreshingly effortless note-taking experience.

As you jot down notes, a timeline naturally forms just for you.

With tags, you can also create timelines centered around specific topics.

Sync quickly and take notes from anywhere.

Open the app and start typing instantly, access strflow from the share sheet, or use a shortcut key on macOS.

All data remains exclusively on your devices and within your iCloud account. We can't access it in any way.

When saved to iCloud, your data is encrypted using your iCloud Keychain. This ensures compliance with Advanced Data Protection, which offers end-to-end encryption.
