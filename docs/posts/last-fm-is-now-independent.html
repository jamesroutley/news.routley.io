What’s happening?

Last.fm is now operating as an independent company following a change in ownership.

Is Last.fm shutting down?

No. The service continues as normal.

No. Last.fm is now operating independently.

The team building and operating Last.fm remains in place.

It means we can focus fully on building listening insights and community features for music fans.
