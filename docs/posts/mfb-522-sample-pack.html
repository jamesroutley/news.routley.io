MFB-522 Sample Pack

Over the last month, I borrowed the excellent MFB-522 Drumcomputer from the Synth Library. The MFB-522 is an all-analogue drum machine that sounds quite similar to the Roland TR-808 except I’ve always thought the hi-hats sounded better.

The unit used to be mine, but I decided more people would get use out of it if I gave it to the Synth Library. But I always regretted not recording its sounds before I did. So when I saw it was available last month, I took to opportunity to check it out :).

You can download the resulting sample pack from my new samples page. I plan on sporadically adding more in the future.
