The Joy of an Empty Brain

Modern distractions cannot compete with the inspiration of old-fashioned boredom

April 21, 2023 9:33 am ET

To write this humor column, I had to empty my brain. I mean empty it even more than its usual echoing, empty state. Let me explain.

Modern life has clogged my skull to the limit. Technology has delivered an avalanche of options to preoccupy me at any hour; the notion of idle time that can’t be filled with some form of digital distraction is foreign to me, almost unnerving.
