'Sur la trace de la Chouette d'Or' (On the trail of the Golden Owl) is a French treasure hunt book, created in 1993 by author Max Valentin and artist Michel Becker. The book provides clues to the location of a buried statuette of an owl. The location of the treasure still remains a mystery 31 years after this book was released. This site presents the clues and evidence to help owl hunters solve this puzzle. Can you solve this armchair treasure hunt?
