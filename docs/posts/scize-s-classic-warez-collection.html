SCiZE's CLASSIC WAREZ COLLECTION

From 1990 onwards I used to roam BBS's, trading and collecting warez.

I've put the filelists from those days online here.

The lists are displayed as they originally were on BBS's with all their FILE_ID.DIZ ascii magic.

I'm very interested in getting in touch with other sceners from the past so if you stumble upon this page please send me an email .
