Unrelated Words Puzzle

How to play

Connect the two unrelated words by adding related words between them.

Each added word must be greater than 20% related to the word above or below.

For example we can connect the unrelated words "puppy" and "bank" with these three added words.

puppy > bark > tree > branch > bank

Relatedness is calculated using word vectors. The calculations are not perfect yet and I'll keep trying to improve the accuracy!
