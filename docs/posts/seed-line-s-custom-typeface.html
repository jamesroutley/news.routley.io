LINE has been conducting global business in various fields around the world. LINE Seed was created in four writing systems starting with Latin, Korean, Japanese, and Thai so that it can be used universally throughout its global network. As a result, we can now deliver our message in a consistent voice.

LINE Seed is a Geometric typeface with some Gothic influences and an emphasis on simplicity. It’s designed to look familiar to everyone in a variety of places. The LINE logo’s straight form has been maintained while also emphasizing clear curves, resulting in a functional typeface with great readability, usability, and legibility.

The value of LINE aims to make the lives of users more convenient through seamless connection with everything including people, information, content, and online and offline services—anytime and anywhere. This value is what sets LINE Seed apart from other typefaces.
