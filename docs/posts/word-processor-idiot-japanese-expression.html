Word-processor idiot (Japanese expression)

Japanese[edit]

Kanji in this term
馬 鹿
ば か
jūbakoyomi

Etymology[edit]

ワープロ + 馬鹿, literally "word-processor idiot".

Noun[edit]

ワープロ馬鹿(バカ) 

  1. Someone who has poor handwriting, especially poor knowledge of writing kanji characters, due to habitual use of Japanese computer text input methods rather than pen and paper.
