Public Staircases

Public staircases are a guilty pleasure of mine. There's one that I use often, a south-facing set of steps that links a pedestrian bridge over the river to our local greenmarket. It's a lovely, sun-drenched place to sit, and when the market's open, its top step is about sixty feet from a Breton couple that makes crepes. 

"a 310 mile, 15 segment stair-walk spanning urban Los Angeles County. The 2018 version of the LA Loop climbs just over 33,000 feet as it traverses 474 stair-streets, and 307 stairways that are not stair-streets, for a total of 781 stairways, with 31,167 total up-steps and 8,804 total down-only stair-steps."
