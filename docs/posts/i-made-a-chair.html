I made possibly the simplest chair from an instructable I found. Uses one 8-foot 2"x12" and some very basic cuts.

It didn't take long, even when the truly incorrect tools of only a circular saw and an oscillating multitool. I sealed up the ends using end cut sealer and called it a day. It works great and honestly I kind of like it better than a lot of my other chairs.
