How it works

Day overview

  • Your entire day is hosted on one screen. No more trying to juggle calendars across multiple tabs
  • Prioritize tasks and block time for them with simple drag’n’drop.
  • Easily switch between days – review your agenda ghosts of past, present, and future.

Collections = categories + tags

  • Group your events, tasks, and notes by projects, people or domains with collections. Now you can easily see how much work the marketing department is sending your way
  • Save time on organizing your knowledge library - just add things to collections as you go through your days. Who needs Dewey Decimal now?

Calendar integration

  • See events from all your calendars in one place. Good-bye forgetting a deadline because you didn’t check that one calendar
  • Easily spot conflicts between your personal and professional schedule, never miss a doctor’s appointment again
  • Schedule new events easily to your calendar

Weekly reports

  • See where your time goes - which collections take most of your time
  • See your busiest days and hope it isn’t Fridays
  • Get reminded about overdue tasks (whoops, that one fell through the cracks)
  • Track your progress in tasks
