Show HN: ReadToMe (iOS) turns paper books into audio

  • Privacy Policy

3-day free trial, then $9.99/month

Turn any book into an audiobook. Scan pages with your phone camera, then listen to high-quality, mistake-free audio.

Scan books or other printed text

Scan up to 20 pages at a time using your phone camera.

Scans -> Audio!

The scans are converted to high-quality audio, without page numbers, footers, etc.

Listen anytime

Use the in-app player to listen to your audio clips whenever you'd like.

Choice of voices

Pick between a selection of high-quality voices to narrate your books to you.

Follow along

After scanning, your book's text is also available in digital form. Read along or use it elsewhere!

Speed-read

Listen at up to 2x speed, or slow your audio down to pay close attention.
