Holistic Wellness

Today I found out that one of my fellow Recursers makes music. No, that's not entirely true: today I found out he makes awesome music. He performs under the moniker Holistic Wellness, and his last work is a three-song serving called “What Time Does the Shock Arrive at the Top of the Pipe?”. This wordy and beautiful piece of sonic deconstruction sneaked up on me, and I loved every second of it. The first song, “Melt Glass”, is a home-made concoction, while the other two songs are covers.

So, without further ado, here's Abe aka Holistic Wellness performing “What Time Does the Shock Arrive at the Top of the Pipe?”. Props if you know where he might derive this monster of a title from.
