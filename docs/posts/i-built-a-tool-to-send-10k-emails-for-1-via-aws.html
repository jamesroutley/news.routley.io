Frequently Asked Questions

How does Mail Layer work?

Mail Layer, our cloud-based solution, operates by eliminating the need for manual installation and maintenance. Simply sign up for a Mail Layer account, input your AWS SES credentials, and start sending campaigns without the hassle of server setup or complex configurations.

Can I import my existing contacts data into Mail Layer?

Yes, you can seamlessly import your existing contacts into Mail Layer. We provide a user-friendly process for importing contacts via CSV data. Additionally, you have the option to manually add contacts, ensuring flexibility in managing your recipient list.

Is there a limit on the number of emails?

Mail Layer offers flexible plans to suit your email sending needs. Our free plan includes 10,000 emails per month. For those requiring more, we have the Solo Plan with a limit of 50,000 emails per month, the Standard Plan with 125,000 emails per month, and the Scale Plan, providing a generous 250,000 emails per month. Choose the plan that aligns with your campaign volume for scalability and cost-effectiveness.

Can I track the performance of my email campaigns?

Yes, Mail Layer provides real-time analytics and reporting features, allowing you to monitor the success of your campaigns, track opens, clicks, and more, right from the dashboard.

Can I customize the appearance of my email campaigns?

Absolutely! Mail Layer provides a user-friendly WYSIWYG editor powered by Tiptap, allowing you to easily customize the appearance of your email campaigns. Design visually engaging and branded emails effortlessly using the available customization features.
