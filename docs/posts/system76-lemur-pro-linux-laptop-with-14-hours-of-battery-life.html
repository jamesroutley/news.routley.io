Lighter than air

From a snappy operating system in Pop!_OS to bloatware-free firmware, this sleek laptop is designed to be lightweight across the board. And at 2.54 lbs (1.15kg), the Lemur Pro is the lightest in its class.

Smooth coding

Quickly compile your code with a 12th Gen Intel Core i7 CPU and up to 40GB RAM. Use all your favorite development tools in a native environment and drastically reduce post-deployment surprises.

Open Source Firmware

See it in the source code: Powered by coreboot, System76 Open Firmware is designed to be light on code for better security. Dive into your day going from cold boot to login in under 10 seconds.

Open Source EC Firmware

Open source embedded controller firmware is our next step towards removing proprietary code from our products entirely. System76 Open EC Firmware is GPLv3 licensed code that grants you access and control over important functionality, such as your keyboard, fans, and battery. Your layout can be easily customized in the OS using the System76 Keyboard Configurator application.

Monitor-Based Docking

Adding a monitor to your order allows you to avoid getting tangled up in an abundance of wires. Through a USB-C connection to any monitor with USB PD support, you can simultaneously charge your laptop and work on two screens.
