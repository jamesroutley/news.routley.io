How to rip audio CDs 2022 – with drive accuracy listing

I tend to keep my audio local. Therefore, I need an accurate ripping device for my audio CD collection. Recently I found a neat listing in the dbPowerAmp forum, but I added some information as well as ways to filter and sort. Have fun!

Hardware listing

Recommendations

  • Try your existing drive before buying one - it may support all required features and it is not that important to have the most accurate one
  • Some of the listed drives are no longer available or very expensive - maybe try to find one in used but good condition, that is not too old or worn out
  • Use Exact Audio Copy (EAC, Windows Only) with the guide’s settings, if you really wanna archive your audio CD content in best possible quality. These improve your copy even more:
    • Follow this guide
    • Error recoery quality: high
    • Secure mode
    • NEVER normalize (!!)
    • DISABLE C2 error correction (even if supported, most drives have bad implementations)
    • Use Accurate Rip
    • Use FLAC (lossless) as destination format
  • If you want to use an external USB drive, buy an internal drive and get a high quality external case for it… this is much better than buying a low accuracy external drive

Best drives:

  • overall: Hitachi-LG BH14NS48 (BD-writer, half-size)
  • slim: hp GUE1N (DVD-writer, slim)
  • slim BluRay Reader / DVD writer: Panasonic UJ-160
