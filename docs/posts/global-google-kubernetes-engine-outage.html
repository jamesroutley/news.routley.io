Global Google Kubernetes Engine Outage

14 Sep 2023 23:31 PDT

Summary: We are experiencing an issue with Google Kubernetes Engine

Description: We are experiencing an issue with Google Kubernetes Engine impacting customers using Kubernetes version 1.25.

Our engineering team continues the mitigation efforts. We do not have a confirmed ETA for completion and will continue to provide regular updates.

We will provide more information by Friday, 2023-09-15 12:30 US/Pacific.

Diagnosis: GKE customers using Kubernetes version 1.25 may experience issues with Persistent Disk creation or deletion failures.

The PersistentVolumeClaim (PVC) may be stuck in pending status because the PersistentVolume (PV) and underlaying disks cannot be created.

The PersistentVolume (PV) deletion may be stuck in deleting status because the underlaying disk cannot be deleted.

Workaround: None at this time.
