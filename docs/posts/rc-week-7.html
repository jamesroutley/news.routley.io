I paused hardware-oriented work this week while waiting for a package of parts to arrive. The few consecutive days not focused solely on my main project was a nice change of pace. I spent more time talking and writing, and as a kind of sacrifice to being public, used my technical focus for most of the week to build this website which I'm now very happy to be writing on.
