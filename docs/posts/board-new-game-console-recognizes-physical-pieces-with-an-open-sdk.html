Board is a 24” tabletop game console that brings people together to play, face-to-face. Every game is controlled by a unique set of pieces, bringing together the tactile feel of board games with the magic of video games.

Power on your Board, choose a game, and follow the built-in tutorial. Every game is controlled by a unique set of pieces that make play intuitive—knives slice, stairs climb, rockets launch.

We believe everyone is a player, from kids and casual players to seasoned gamers. Board is designed for every skill level and every group size, whether you’re playing solo, teaming up, or competing with friends.

The Board and most games are designed for ages 6+, and you can set parental controls to help manage playtime. No content is mature.

Board includes a library of 12 games for every skill level, designed exclusively for Board by the best game studios. Our library includes arcade, strategy, action, and more.

Board fuses the best of board games and video games into something entirely new. It’s social, tactile, and endlessly replayable, all designed to be played around the table.
