Show HN: Sorbay – Open-source alternative to Loom

What can I do with Sorbay?

Sorbay allows you to quickly create and share screen- and camera recordings. It comes in two parts: A desktop client and a backend service to store, organize and share recordings.

  • Desktop App available for macOS, Windows and Linux.
  • Videos are streamed to the backend while recording and can be shared as soon as you are finished.
  • Open Source: Stay secure and compliant by having full control of all your recordings.

Pricing

Sorbay is currently in beta. We plan on funding development by charging for a cloud hosted service and an enterprise on-prem release.

Team

Jannis and Alex met in 2020 during the lockdown with an initial focus on building a startup for a different idea. During the build process a lot of their communication was done via screen recordings.

Being a long time Open Source advocate, Jannis pushed for a self-hosted screen recording option. However, there was nothing available that gave the simple experience they were looking for. This is when the idea of Sorbay was born.

Jannis has built a successful Open Source startup before while Alex brings a cross between product and marketing to help push Sorbay's growth.
