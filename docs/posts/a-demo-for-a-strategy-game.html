Show HN: A Demo for a Strategy Game

About This Demo

The Empire of Rephsis teeters on the brink of collapse. As the Duke of Histria, you watch as Emperor Lucius Vetus's grip on power weakens. Will you seize this opportunity to claim the throne, or will you fall victim to the machinations of your rivals?

Plot your path to the throne in the demo for Fall of an Empire. Set in the declining Empire of Rephsis, you play as the ambitious Duke of Histria, plotting your ascension to Imperial power. Navigate treacherous political scenarios, outmaneuver your enemies, and ultimately seize control of the empire before it crumbles.

Chart your rise to power

In this prologue, you'll experience the journey from provincial ruler to Emperor. Build your power base, forge alliances, and eliminate threats as you climb the ladder of imperial politics. Your decisions will shape the future of Rephsis and set the stage for the challenges you'll face in the full game.

Expand your influence

Command your ducal forces and expand your reach. Recruit armies, engage in strategic battles, and prove your military prowess to gain the respect of your peers and the fear of your enemies.

Provincial management

Learn the ropes of governance by managing your duchy. Develop your holdings, balance your economy, and keep your subjects loyal as you prepare for ever-greater power.

Political intrigue

Navigate a web of alliances and rivalries among the empire's noble houses. Make calculated decisions to gain supporters and undermine your opponents in your quest for the throne.

Strategic warfare

Master the art of warfare through the game's unique timed turn-based battle system. Lead your troops to victory and prove yourself worthy of the imperial crown.

Rich narrative

Immerse yourself in the lore and history of Rephsis. Uncover the weaknesses of the regime and exploit them to your advantage.

Do you have what it takes to seize the throne and face the challenges that lie ahead?
