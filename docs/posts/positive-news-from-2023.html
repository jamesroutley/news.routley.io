Many bad things happened in 2023. You already know them. Many tragic events were so terrible we will never forget them and we shouldn’t. You can easily find them all over the news. But the good things that happened are much harder to encounter, and it is easy to get the impression nothing good happened at all. In order to keep struggling to make the world better, we all need some hope. Therefore we have collected a list of 100 positive stories from 2023 that might make you feel differently about the 12 months that went by! With these things in mind, it is easier to imagine that 2024 can become better!
