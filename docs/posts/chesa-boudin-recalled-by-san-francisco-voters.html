Chesa Boudin Recalled by San Francisco Voters

Updated June 8, 2022 1:12 am ET

SAN FRANCISCO—San Francisco District Attorney Chesa Boudin was recalled by voters Tuesday, according to an Associated Press projection, in a blow to the progressive prosecutors movement.

Voters strongly endorsed ousting the reform-minded D.A., with 61.3% supporting the recall after an estimated 45% of votes were counted, the Associated Press said.
