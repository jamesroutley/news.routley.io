I was 17 and had free period before and after lunch. So, 3 hour lunches.

I went to Whole Foods and left my keys locked in my car.

You can’t break into the backseat window with a rock. The glass is proof against that. A lady saw me and didn’t flip, already a good sign. While I rooted around for anything I could jimmy the car with, I talked with her.

I don’t remember what we talked about and it eats me because while it was trivial, it mattered to me that she could care so freely. She was very taken with me, and not only offered to call AAA, but even insisted on paying for half. Struck me so much I feel a duty to remember her and write her down.

Triple AAA guy used this air pump to make the door open just enough to snake in this wire hanger thing.

I hope you’re well lady. Wish I remembered your name.
