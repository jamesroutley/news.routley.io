LineByLine: Memorize Anything

Memorize Anything

Have you ever wanted to memorize a favorite poem, speech, or section of scripture? This site will help you commit any text to memory permanently and easily.

Try it now!

How it works

  1. Choose a text to memorize and type or paste it into this form. Not sure what to memorize? We have a list of sample texts from famous poets and authors.
  2. Review your text. Just a few minutes each day is enough. LineByLine will test and keep track of how well you know each line. As your memory improves, more letters from each line disappear and more lines are unlocked. Before you know it, you'll be reciting the entire text from heart!
  3. Memorize something new! LineByLine uses spaced repetition to keep track of how well you know each line to make efficient use of your review time.

Start memorizing!
