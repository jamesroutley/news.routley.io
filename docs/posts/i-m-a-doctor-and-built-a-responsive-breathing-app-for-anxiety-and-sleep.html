Ratings and Reviews

Great for sleeping

Sometimes I wake up in the middle of the night and can’t go back to sleep. This app helps a lot to stop my mind from wandering around. The new feature, where it makes small vibrations signaling to inhale, exhale or hold, is fantastic. I can keep it in my hands and not even look at the screen until I fall asleep. Maybe they can use a different signal for inhale vs. exhale. Because sometimes, I fall asleep for a few seconds and then don’t know if the vibration is for exhaling or inhale without looking at the screen. Overall I highly recommend it.

Thank you so much for the review! We're delighted to hear Lungy is helping you and that the new haptics are useful. We'll look into making the vibrations more specific for each breath phase – look out for an update soon :)

My favorite breathwork app

I tried so many breathwork apps before this one. I love the design. It’s simple, visually pleasing, and easy to navigate. The animations are interesting and I like that you also have the basic breathwork visuals like the filling circle or dot on a path. The whole experience is dynamic yet soothing.

Helpful and works

Still the best breathing app to have hands down. I use this daily and during tough times. Thanks for making it so nice to look at as well. Plenty to learn about breathing as well. Simply an amazing app.
