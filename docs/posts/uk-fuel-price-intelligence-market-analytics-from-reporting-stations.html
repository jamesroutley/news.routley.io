UK Fuel Price Intelligence – Market analytics from reporting stations

Built by an independent developer. Not affiliated with the UK government, the Fuel Finder scheme, or any fuel retailer. About this site

Contains public sector information licensed under the Open Government Licence v3.0. Price data sourced from the UK Fuel Finder scheme, operated under the Data (Use and Access) Act 2025 and enforced by the Competition and Markets Authority (CMA). Station operators are legally required to report price changes within 30 minutes. This platform stores and analyses publicly mandated data. We do not set, influence, or editorially adjust any prices.
