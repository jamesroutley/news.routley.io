Lego Ideas Boeing 747 Cockpit Achieves 10k Supporters

The Boeing 747 Cockpit by DarrenJones is the latest project to achieve 10,000 supporters on LEGO Ideas. The 1,874 piece build features a mostly faithful recreation of the cockpit found in a Boeing 747.

The project now joins The Forestmen Secret Inn, The Legendary Concorde, Wild West Mine, LEGO Koala, LEGO Toilet, Mini City Diorama: London with Underground Station!, Steampunk Airship, The Kit-Cat Clock, Tales of the Space Age, The Library, Santa’s Cottage, Mech Patrol, and The Polar Express – 20th Anniversary as the ones to reach the First 2022 Review Stage.
