How does Seraph Secure protect me?

Seraph Secure keeps you and your loved ones safe from scammers accessing your computer and from falling victim to malicious websites and phishing attempts. Our software monitors what’s happening on your computer to detect scam activity including remote connection software, utility abuse, scam websites and then provide real time alerts to your assigned friend or family member.

What devices will Seraph Secure work on?

Currently Seraph Secure works on Windows 10 & 11 operating systems. Mac OS/iOS is already in development and expected to be released in the near future.

Does Seraph Secure collect my information and monitor my browsing history?

Seraph has your privacy in mind and aims to protect it. In order to keep you safe, Seraph Secure does monitor web activity, however, we do not log, track or sell your data. If you do visit a scam site, we will store that site so your Guardian can be notified. Anything that is not related to a scam will not be stored or tracked.  See privacy policy.

Won’t my antivirus software protect me?

Most antivirus software is predominantly focused on viruses and malware, however, they cannot keep scammers from connecting to your computer. Seraph Secure was developed to protect you from scams. Unlike antivirus software, Seraph Secure is not a browser extension. Our software runs in the background of your computer so it is able to scan processes and detect specific activity scammers use. If you have an antivirus program already, Seraph is not a replacement for that but can work in conjunction with it.
