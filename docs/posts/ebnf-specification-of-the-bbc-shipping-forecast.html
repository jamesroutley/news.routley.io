Show HN: EBNF Specification of the BBC Shipping Forecast

The BBC Shipping Forecast has a very strict format. This repo contains a specification for Shipping Forecasts in EBNF.

Actual shipping forecasts will deviate from these rules - there's a bit more wiggle room in the general synopsis and the wording of the area forecasts. Nonetheless, this specification captures the general structure and will cover most parts of most forecasts.
