Where exactly are our production breakdowns happening?

Cerrion tracks every visually-distinguishable anomaly on your production line and aggeregates the data by region in a central dashboard. Enabling your production teams to focus their attention on the most problematic regions.

Why are breakdowns happening?

Cerrion provides production teams with anonymized and highlighted video snippets of every anomaly. Production engineer, technicians and supervisors can watch footage of the problem at different speeds to understand what has happend in a matter of minutes.

Are our actions reducing breakdowns?

Eliminate guess-work and provide your teams with  relaible data to measure the outcomes of their actions. With Cerrion, line supervisors can see how the anomaly count per region and severity is progressing.
