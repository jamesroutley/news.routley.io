The Interstellar Style of Sun Ra (2016)

He was born on Saturn. Or he was born nowhere (he preferred not to discuss his origin story in terra firma terms). Or, as a birth certificate for Herman Poole Blount claims, he was born in Birmingham, Alabama, on May 22, 1914. This detail is confirmed by his biographer, John Szwed, but for the better part of his time on Earth, Sun Ra avoided the literal world. He focused instead on the ancient times of his imagination, the future realms, and above all, music: His wildly innovative, improvised, modal, cascading, sometimes dissonant but also frequently harmonious compositions worked in touches of ragtime, big band, swing, bebop, and, of course, free jazz, of which he was a pioneer.
