Q. Is this just a contact form?

A. No. When a user creates a ticket on your support page it appears on your support dashboard. At that point you, or the user, can add comments. Each participant is notified via email when a new reply is added to the conversation.

Q. How many support agents can I have?

A. We're trying to do things a little differently with this product. You can have as many agents as you like. No limits.

Q. How customisable is the support page?

A. Quite. Right now you can easily style the page up with your own brand colors. If demand is high enough we'll add the ability incorporate your own logo.
