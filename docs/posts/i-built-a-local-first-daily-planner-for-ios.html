Show HN: I built a local-first daily planner for iOS

Calendar, Reminder, Schedule

Only for iPhone

Free · In-App Purchases · Designed for iPhone

Schedule your tasks to calendar in just few taps and speed up your daily planning. Key features: Session: Group multiple tasks within a single calendar event. Highlight: Plan your important tasks effortlessly. Status: Quickly organize tasks by their status. Task Progress: Automatically update your progress as you complete subtasks. Calendar Groups: Filter events from specific calendar sets to keep your schedule clutter-free.
