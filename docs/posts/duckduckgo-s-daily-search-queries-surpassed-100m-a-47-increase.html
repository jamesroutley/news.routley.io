DuckDuckGo's daily search queries surpassed 100M, a 47% increase

DuckDuckGo’s daily search queries have surpassed 100 million, with a 47 percent increase expected in 2021.

DuckDuckGo, a search engine with enhanced privacy protection, is currently gaining popularity.

According to the company, daily search queries have surpassed 100 million.

It has grown by nearly 47 percent in just 2021.

The Search Engine DuckDuckGo Is Getting Bigger

The rising platform has shown a significant improvement in terms of year-over-year growth, according to a report published over the weekend by Bleeping Computer.

Despite Google’s search engine dominance, many people are experimenting with the privacy-focused platform.

DuckDuckGo received 79 million daily search queries on average last year in this month.

In addition, the platform’s total search queries were 23.6 billion at the time.

The Pennsylvania-based company received an average of 100 million daily search queries in 2021.

The total number of search queries has reached 34.6 billion so far, with four days until the year 2022 begins.

This year, the team grew by 46.4 percent.

DuckDuckGo Mac Version Coming Soon

DuckDuckGo has a low market share.

According to the same report from Bleeping Computer, there’s still a lot to see for the DuckDuckGo search engine’s progress.

Despite strong growth this year, its market share still trails that of its competitors.

With that, it only has a 2.53 percent share of the total market, which is slightly less than Yahoo’s 3.3 percent and Bing’s 6.43 percent.

Furthermore, Google remains the most popular internet search engine in the United States, accounting for 87.33 percent of all search traffic.

The DuckDuckGo desktop web browser is currently in development, according to Tech Times.

In a recent blog post, the company’s CEO, Gabriel Weinberg, confirmed this.

Google vs. DuckDuckGo (DuckDuckGo vs. Google)

According to a separate report from Spread Privacy, Google will track users when they use the search engine, whereas DuckDuckGo will not.

The company guarantees that the information of its users will be kept safe and private when using this new platform.

The general perception of these platforms is that your data is mined and tracked for advertising purposes.

This explains why you see ads that are very similar to your interests.

Users will be protected from email tracking by the privacy-focused search engine’s “Email Protection.”

Scammers who want to track your actual email address for malicious purposes won’t be able to do so now.

DuckDuckGo’s Android ecosystem includes “App Tracking” protection, which prevents third-party trackers from being used.
