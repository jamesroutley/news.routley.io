Show HN: Can you tell if an image is AI-generated?

Welcome to the human recognition of AI-generated imagery research quiz, or AI Quiz for short. In this quiz, you'll be shown 30 images (photographs and artwork) and asked whether each image was generated by an AI program or authored by a human. These results will be compiled and published in mid to late December of 2022.

This quiz should take less than 15 minutes. Thanks for your participation.
