TruthWave – A platform for corporate whistleblowers

Our Beta is Now Live!

Welcome to the platform and community for those who bring unethical corporations to justice.

For far too long, harmful corporate culture has stigmatized and disincentivized information flow. TruthWave is rewriting this narrative by creating a platform that financially compensates whistleblowers for courageously stepping forward while leveraging a global community built around justice.

At its core, TruthWave is an information platform that allows those who possess or locate vital information about corporate wrongdoing to share it securely and anonymously.

TruthWave makes it easy to be courageous and hold wrongdoers accountable. All you need to do is send us a message through one of our secure channels.

While we make it easy to create Tips, we constantly collaborate with you throughout your journey. Through trusted, end-to-end encrypted communications, our team stays with you every step of the way.

We provide a secure and anonymous experience for internal whistleblowers and external researchers who share information of corporate wrongdoing.  We don't ever know a Tipper's true identity unless they explicitly choose to disclose it.

With our unique financial rewards model, scale matters. The more justice you unlock, the more monetary compensation you receive.
