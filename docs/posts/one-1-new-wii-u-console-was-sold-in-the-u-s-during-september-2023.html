One (1) new Wii U console was sold in the U.S. during September 2023

Circana’s (formerly The NPD Group) Mat Piscatella has relayed some new sales data for the United States for the month of September, indicating that a single Wii U console was sold in the country. This marks the first time a brand new Wii U console has been sold since May 2022. It remains unclear which retailer sold the Wii U, given that the console was discontinued a few years ago. It is possible that it was a misplaced unit that was rediscovered by the retailer.
