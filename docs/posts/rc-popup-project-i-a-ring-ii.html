RC Popup, Project I: A Ring II

This post is going to be quite short, just a little update on the ring project I talked about yesterday and a quick note on some other tinkerings. I’ve not been able to make much headway today, because I still get a lot of e-mails after my blog post on Carp was on Hackernews and r/programming. I enjoy these messages, but they are taking a toll on my productivity at the moment.

Fig. 1: Now that looks like a ring!
