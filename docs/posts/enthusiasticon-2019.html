EnthusiastiCon 2019

I’ve got great news for all of you: EnthusiastiCon is back in 2019! The website is looking sharper than ever and the CFP is open for another two weeks, what more can you ask for?

But before we get ahead of ourselves, a few bits of information for those of you that don’t know EnthusiastiCon yet: EnthusiastiCon is a two-day, single-track conference in Berlin. Its core idea is based on !!con, which means that talks will only be around 10 minutes long, and about a topic that really excites the speaker. We’ve had talks about topics as diverse as spreadsheets, type systems, LED clothing, machine learning, and self care!

Thanks as always to our sponsors, Port Zero and the Recurse Center, and to our hosts, ThoughtWorks and Co-Up!

See you there!
