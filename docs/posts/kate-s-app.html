What Is Kate's App?

Kate's App is a tool created to support medical caregivers and the people they care for. With Kate's App, you can share and coordinate medical information, and to do it securely and efficiently, with attention to privacy, user permissions, and collaboration.

Why Kate's App?

Sometimes medical care can be overwhelming and needs to be shared. Chronic pain. Fatigue. Discouragement. You can help the people who want to help you.
