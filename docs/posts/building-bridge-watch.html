Building Bridge Watch

Written by Maryanne Wachter and Aaron Freidenberg

You can follow Maryanne on Twitter.

  1. West Seattle Bridge Case Study

    February 01, 2022

    A case study of the West Seattle Bridge, a post-tensioned cantilever segmental bridge. The bridge has been closed to traffic since March 2020 due to the discovery of substantial cracks in the center span of the structure. Also a demonstration of how to use Bridge.watch to locate similar bridges within the U.S.

  2. Building Bridge Watch

    January 31, 2022

    Brief overview of the methodology and microservices powering Bridge.watch, an interactive data visualization platform for bridge infrastructure within the U.S. based on the FHWA's National Bridge Inventory.

Bridge.watch

Bridge.watch provides interactive data visualization for open-access data records from the FHWA of bridges located in the United States.
