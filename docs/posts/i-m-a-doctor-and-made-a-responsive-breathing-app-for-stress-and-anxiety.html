Breathing exercises that respond to you.

Breathing exercises can help you to feel happier & healthier, but it can be tricky to do them every day and get the full benefits – that's where Lungy comes in!

Lungy uses your smartphone to recognise and respond to your breathing, creating a real-time audiovisual experience that evolves with you. No more following timers or pre-recorded instructions - every breath is new, exciting and rewarding!

Relax with Nature.

Lungy was developed to make breathing exercises more engaging and fun. By giving you nature-inspired visuals & music to focus on, Lungy can help you stay present and mindful in the moment.

Developed by doctors for sleep, stress & sports.

Lungy was developed by doctors to guide you through breathing exercises, and to make them more engaging and fun. Lungy gives you nature-inspired visuals to focus on, helping you stay present and mindful, as a form of active meditation.

Lungy uses its own game-engine and patent-pending technology to create real-time generative graphics and sound. No videos, no pre-recorded audio, it’s an evolving experience that is unique and unfolds with each breath.

See your exercise data.

Lungy presents your exercise data in a way that is easy to understand, and can give you tips on how to use Lungy more effectively.

What volume did Lungy measure during the exercise – was it the volume of a tennis ball or a watermelon?! Have you managed to breathe out the volume of an elephant? Lungy can answer all these important questions and more.
