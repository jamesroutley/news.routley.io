Show HN: Timbre – An app that finds the best sounds for your sleep

iPhone Screenshots

Experience a new level of relaxation with Timbre, the ultimate sleep companion app. Dive into a world of curated soundscapes, sleep sound rankings, and sleep feedback to transform your sleep routine and enhance your overall well-being.

Made alarm feature available to all users, with Smart Wake-up limited to Pro users. Includes other bug fixes and performance improvements.
