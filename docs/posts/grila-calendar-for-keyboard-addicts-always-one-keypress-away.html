Show HN: Grila – Calendar for keyboard addicts, always one keypress away

Press ⏎ enter to show a color-coded list of events and reminders starting from the selected date.

Separate calendars and reminder lists can be disabled by pressing the , (comma) key to open the settings window.

Grila can't and won't modify, add or delete events in your calendars and reminder lists.

You can go fast and reckless with your typing, there's nothing to break.

On birthday events, the app will show the contact photo and will hide possible duplicated events for a cleaner look.

Recurring events will show how often they happen in an easily readable form (e.g. every 1 month) and colored dots under each day can help you see future holidays and free time at a glance.
