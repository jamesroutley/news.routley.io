Website to Sign Up for the Lyme Disease Vaccine Phase 3 Trial

Participate in a Clinical Research Study

Do you or your family spend a lot of time outside? Help protect you and your family from Lyme Disease by participating in a research study.

Lyme disease is caused by bacteria found in ticks. When a tick bites a person or animal, it leaves behind harmful bacteria that can cause Lyme disease and other illnesses.

We are currently seeking participants 5 years and older who spend a lot of time outdoors and who are at risk of getting Lyme Disease to participate in a clinical research study. The purpose of the study is to research the effectiveness of the study vaccine in hopes that it will reduce the possibility of people getting Lyme disease.

  • This study is testing an investigational vaccine to see if it can help prevent Lyme disease in children and adults.
  • Participation is always voluntary. You can leave the study at any time.

You may qualify to participate in this study if you:

  • Live in an area where there are a lot of ticks that carry Lyme disease
  • Are a child or teen age 5-17 and have permission from your parent or guardian
  • Are an adult age 18 or older
  • Access cutting-edge investigational treatments
  • Receive free study-related care
  • Save or improve lives
  • Speak to medical experts
  • Compensation for time and travel
