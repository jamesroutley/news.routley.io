Show HN: Jobs by Referral: Find jobs in your LinkedIn network

JobsByReferral analyzes your professional network to find job openings at companies where you have connections. Get referred by people you already know and dramatically increase your chances of landing interviews.

What's it look like?

1

Get your connections data

You can request a data archive from your professional network provider if you have one, or prepare a CSV file with your connections in the following format:

First Name, Last Name, URL, Email Address, Company, Position, Connected On

John, Smith, https://example.com/profile, john@email.com, Google, Software Engineer, 15 Jan 2024

• Required fields: First Name, Last Name, Company, Position

• Optional fields: URL, Email Address, Connected On

• The app will analyze which companies have the most connections in your network

2

Want to try it with a demo export?

Don't have your professional network data yet? Try JobsByReferral with our sample dataset to see how it works.
