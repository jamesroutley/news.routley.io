PICKING UP A ZILLION PIECES OF LITTER
I have a condition that I refer to as "Environmental OCD". (When it really bothers you to see litter on the ground.) But instead of getting upset about litter and complaining that someone ought to do something about it, I realize that I am "someone", and I am proud to be among those who are doing something about it.
