Particle Lenia: Self Organising Particles

Particle Lenia is an Artificial Lifeform that consists of point particles that simultaneously generate a potential energy field, and try to minimize their own local energy. Just a few parameters create a huge variety of different “creatures”.

We invite you to catch a few of them at https://znah.net/lenia and share. PNGs can be brought to life if you drop them into the demo window!

Or enjoy a few exemplars we put on display in this video:

The main inspiration comes from Flow Lenia. We decided to preserve the system mass by simply replacing the scalar field representing creatures with a population of particles. Great thanks to
