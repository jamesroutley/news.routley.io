Eggs are 60% more expensive than last year in the US

Overall, however, inflation in the U.S. cooled in December, Thursday's numbers showed.

The overall 12-month Consumer Price Index was up 6.5% in December. That's welcome news for the Federal Reserve and White House, since inflation was at 7.1% in November, a decline largely driven by drops in gas prices and airfare.

Eggs are an outlier, The Washington Post reported, because the industry is still working through supply chain challenges and facing a deadly bird flu outbreak.

Shoppers have begun to take note of the price hikes. Google searches for "egg prices" are at their highest level in five years, Google Trends data shows.
