Show HN: I made a browser-based RTS game

Step in to the public beta of Battle of Flags, a fun browser-based real-time strategy (RTS) game. Collect resources, build towers and train archers and swordsmen to destroy your enemies' forts in both custom and ranked 2-4 player matches.

You can also create your own maps in the mapeditor and play on them with your friends!

No download or installation required!
