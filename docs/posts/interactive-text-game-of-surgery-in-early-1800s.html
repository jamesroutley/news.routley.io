Interactive text game of surgery in early 1800s

Step into the role of a surgeon at St Thomas’ Hospital in the early 19th century, before the introduction of anaesthetics and antiseptic practices. 

Consider the difficult decisions facing a surgeon and choose your path. Will your patient live?

Warning: This story is inspired by a real historical case and contains graphic descriptions of injury and surgery.
