Pico8 Music Synthesizer

Welcome to picoSYNTH! It is a playful way to create simple loops and beats. But by combining the equipment in interesting ways you can also make crazy complex stuff. Hope you'll enjoy it!

Features

  • Build rigs in playful and straightforward ways
  • Make music and loops
  • Live tuning of sounds and beats
  • Choose from multiple waveforms and equipment 
  • Wire up your creations for unexpected results
  • Save files in easy to read text format
  • Export to WAV and import everywhere

Quickstart

  • Use the [+] menu to create add equipment to the scene
  • Drag and drop things on the trashcan to delete it
  • Use cables to connect different equipment
  • You cannot have more than 4 speakers
  • Read the manual and experiment!
