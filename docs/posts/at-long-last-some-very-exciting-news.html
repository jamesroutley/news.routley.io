At long last, some very exciting news

Hello from a positively wild weather week. Something magical about sweating profusely in the littlest of clothing to getting drenched by a magnificent thunderstorm and feeling a little, dare I say, chilly, all in the span of four hours. 

I’m writing today with a bit of NEWS from my little corner of the world. At long last, I no longer feel like I’m going to jinx anything if I tell you that come fall of this year, First Bloom will become a permanent resident of The Big Apple. 

Subscribe to a newsletter

To everyone who’s appreciated First Bloom thus far, who’s made the trek to Bloomville (which is still very much open!), who came to the pop-up in the city last winter: Thank you. This is happening because you came and bought a few tins of fish or several bottles of olive oil or appreciated the produce enough to take photos like it was a department store holiday window display. Your enthusiasm, encouragement and excitement for what I think is a very special little place is the biggest reason that this grocery dream is soon to be a fabulous reality. 

I don’t have more to share at the moment– I think it’s just a little premature– but trust me, there will be plenty of time for me to tell you all about this wild ride and update you on what’s to come. Who knows, I might even “Vlog about it.” That said, as much as I love to keep secrets until the last possible moment, I do need to hire some really wonderful people, so: I’m posting a few job descriptions in hopes of getting some really wonderful applications.
