Innovative Camera System

Smarter, clearer, more secure

To call Zenbook 17 Fold OLED smart is an understatement: it’s packed with some of the latest intelligent convenience and security features to make your life easier, safer and more productive.

5 MP AI camera

ASUS 3DNR technology

Our HD infrared camera with AdaptiveLock is powered by the new Intel Visual Sensing Controller. This AI-powered feature senses when you move away from the laptop and locks the laptop for security, and logs right back in when you approach the laptop. When you look away from the screen, it also dims the screen to save battery life.

There’s also a color sensor that detects ambient light levels and automatically adjusts the brightness and color temperature of the screen to provide the best-looking image for your surroundings.

The 5 MP AI webcam features ASUS 3D Noise Reduction (3DNR) technology to give you superbly clear video calls. The unique algorithm sharpens images and performs real-time de-noising to make sure everything looks crisp and clear during video calls.

Mode Switcher lets you choose up to three windows via the icon at the lower right of the display. Windows are automatically arranged to fit the folded screen.
