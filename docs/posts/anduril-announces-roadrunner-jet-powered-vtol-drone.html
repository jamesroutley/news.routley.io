Twin-Turbojet VTOL Autonomous Air Vehicle (AAV)

Roadrunner

Roadrunner is a reusable, vertical take-off and landing (VTOL), operator-supervised Autonomous Air Vehicle (AAV) with twin turbojet engines and modular payload configurations that can support a variety of missions.

Launch, Loiter, Return to Base

Vertical take-off and landing (VTOL) capability gives Roadrunner the flexibility to rapidly launch from and return to any location for recovery and reuse at near-zero cost.

High Performance Flight

Roadrunner is powered by twin-turbojet engines that enable high subsonic speed and high G-force maneuverability.

Autonomy

Roadrunner leverages cutting-edge advancements in AI and autonomy to enable a single operator to supervise many Roadrunners simultaneously.

Interoperable with In-Service Capabilities

Roadrunner

With high-subsonic speeds, extreme maneuverability, modular payloads, and continuous software upgrades, Roadrunner can accomplish a wide variety of missions today while enabling flexibility for mission sets of tomorrow.

Roadrunner-M

Roadrunner-M can take off, follow, and intercept distant targets at the first hint of danger, giving operators more information and time to assess the target and rules of engagement.

Nest

Nest is a networked, automated hangar for easy transport, maintenance, and launch of Roadrunner. Built with integrated environmental stability systems and health monitoring, Nest ensures Roadrunner is always ready to launch within seconds.
