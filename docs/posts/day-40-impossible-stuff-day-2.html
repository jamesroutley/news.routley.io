Notes

  • For my second Impossible Stuff Day I started a new project, Skyspace Locator. My goal is to plot all of James Turrell’s skyspaces on a map. I was able to get the project set up and go through the Leaflet quick start guide to add a map with two markers showing the two NYC skyspaces.
  • It was a lot of fun! I love maps and I love all the puns in the map world like maptime and mappy hour.
  • Next steps are to create a spreadsheet with all the skyspaces and convert it to GeoJSON, then use that to add the markers.
  • “Do not attempt to download the planet in a web browser.” – OpenStreetMap Wiki
  • TIL there is a new skyspace in NYC that just opened last year! I can’t wait to visit someday.
