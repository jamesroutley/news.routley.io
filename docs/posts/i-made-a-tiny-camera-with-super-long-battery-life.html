the fine print

  1. Photon's low-light performance is a work in progress.
  2. Photon's motion sensor can't detect motion behind glass, due to the limitations of the underlying PIR technology.
  3. When placed in nature, Photon's motion sensor tends to trigger often due to grass or leaves blowing in the wind, which can cause Photon's battery to deplete quickly if configured to capture a photo when motion occurs.
