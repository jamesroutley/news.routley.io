Meeting Scheduler

Finding meeting times are painful. Meetsy simplifies finding a time that works for you and your friend!

How Meetsy Works

Sign in with Google.
Simply login using your most used Google account so Meetsy can find available time slots

Create an event.
Within a couple of clicks, set up an event with potential times.

Share & select a time.
Share your event with friends via a link. Meetsy will help book a time that suits them and you.

Meetsy takes two calendars and finds the best times to meet up!
