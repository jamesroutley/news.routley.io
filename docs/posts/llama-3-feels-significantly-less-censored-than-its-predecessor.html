Llama 3 feels significantly less censored than its predecessor

Llama 3 feels significantly less censored than its predecessor.

The Llama 3 models have substantially lower false refusal rates, with less than a third of the prompts previously refused by Llama 2 now being accepted, making it possible to discuss a wider range of interesting topics!
