Frequently asked questions

What is this?

Gumtrends is a dataset of 25k+ Gumroad products. The dataset contains useful information like estimated revenue, ratio of mixed reviews, number of sales and more.

What is this for?

Our data is useful in different ways:

What are mixed reviews?

Mixed reviews are 2, 3 or 4 star reviews. They usually mean that a product could be improved.

Why should I care about mixed reviews?

Looking for mixed reviews is a known technique for spotting opportunities when doing research for a possible product. When combined with a high number of sales and/or revenue, they are a strong indicator that there's a market in that niche and even products that are not eccellent are selling a lot.

How is this better than just browsing Gumroad?

- We let you sort by % of mixed reviews or number of 3 star reviews

  • Access to the full 25k+ products database
  • Revenue, sales, ratings filters
  • Lifetime access
  • No subscription
