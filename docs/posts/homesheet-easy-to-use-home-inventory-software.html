Show HN: HomeSheet, easy to use home inventory software

What are some practical uses for HomeSheet?

Home Organization

Never lose a receipt again. HomeSheet makes it easy to store your receipts, warranty cards, and manuals directly with your items. Track serial numbers so they're handy if your devices ever get stolen.

Vehicle Management

Use HomeSheet to keep track of receipts and warranties for parts, vehicle manuals, and more. Plus, you can schedule maintenance reminders for important tasks.

Appliance Maintenance Tracking

HomeSheet makes it easy to protect your appliances by sending you reminders to complete necessary maintenance. If you forget to replace your furnace filter regularly, then HomeSheet is for you.

Collection Management

Board Game, Book, or Sports collection? Use HomeSheet to inventory your collections and track their values. Plus, you'll have an inventory to share with your insurance company should they ever be lost or stolen.
