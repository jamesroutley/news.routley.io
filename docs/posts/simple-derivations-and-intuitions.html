variance = expected squared distance from mean, squared because it gives us nice properties :))

Chebyshev's inequality = variance and the values of a random variable are tied together, knowing the variance let's you upper bound the probability of values at the tails occurring

Kelly criterion = the optimal way to size your bets if you have a prior on how the coin flips, rooted in maximizing the logarithmic value of wealth, $2p - 1$ for doubling stake/losing it
