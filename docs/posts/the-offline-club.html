Why people join The Offline Club

We host a variety of offline events and experiences - from offline cafe hangouts to phone-free dinners with strangers. Here's why people join:

1.

To have fun and meaningful conversations in a space with people open to connect.

2.

To read, draw, write and spend relaxed alone-time surrounded by like-minded others. 

3.

To take a break from screens and social media, focus, and get creative.
