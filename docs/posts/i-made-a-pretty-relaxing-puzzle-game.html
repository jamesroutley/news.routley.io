How to play?

  • Click on any tile to rotate it 90°
  • At the beginning, each tile is randomly rotated by 90°, 180° or 270°
  • You win once you rotate all tiles into correct position
  • To achieve the ideal click count you can switch the rotation direction at any time by clicking on or
  • If you don't know what to do next, click on to preview the final picture for 3 seconds, it will "cost" you 1 extra click
