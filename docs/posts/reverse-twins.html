Reverse Twins

2nd October 2025

Fun Fact

37∣73

If two people’s ages differ by a multiple of 9, then every 11 years their ages will be mirrored.

For example, my brother turned 37 while my mom was 73. My mom, being a lover of numbers, immediately pointed out this fun symmetry.

And we realized this “reverse twin” phenomenon has happened every 11 years, when their ages were:

04∣4015∣5126∣6237∣73

But my age and my mom’s age always differ by either 35 years (between our birthdays) or 34 years, neither of which is a multiple of 9, so we will never be reverse twins.

😭∣😭

Proof

XY∣YX

The base-10 number “XY” represents the value 10X+Y and its reverse “YX” represents 10Y+X.

So the difference between “XY” and “YX” is:

(10X+Y)−(10Y+X)=9X−9Y=9(X−Y)

That is, the difference between reversed decimal numbers is always a multiple of 9.

Meanwhile, changing a number by 11 changes its tens digit and ones digit by the same amount, so the symmetry remains.
