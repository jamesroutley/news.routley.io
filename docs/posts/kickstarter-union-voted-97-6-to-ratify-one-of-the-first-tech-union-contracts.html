Kickstarter Union voted 97.6% to ratify one of the first tech union contracts

It’s official! 97.6% of our union has voted YES to ratify our first collective bargaining agreement. This contract will improve our members' lives, make Kickstarter a more equitable workplace, and help raise standards across the tech industry. Here are some highlights 🧵

We fought hard for a contract that prioritizes security and wellness amidst rising inflation, uncertainty, and burnout. We: 💰 Revamped our compensation structure 🔒 Improved professional development and job security 🧑🏽‍🦽 Expanded wellness benefits, including leaves of absence

We set out to make Kickstarter a more equitable place. We approached every proposal with an eye towards equity. Some standout wins include: ⚖️ Annual pay equity reviews 🌎 No more geographic adjustments for pay 💪🏾 Anti-discrimination clause that goes *far* above the legal minimum

We know that our win will have ripples across the tech sector, and we hope to see some things become standard provisions, including: 🕵🏻‍♂️ No intrusive monitoring ⏰ Time in lieu for any hours worked beyond a full work week 🤏🏽 Limits on the use of subcontractors
