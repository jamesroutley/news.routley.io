The New Collabora Office for Desktop

Collabora Online now on desktop

We’re excited to share the first release of the new Collabora Office for desktop – bringing the familiar, powerful Collabora Online experience, to run locally on Windows, macOS, and Linux.

Open it and feel instantly at home: ODF or DOCX, quick edits or deep dives, a clean, beautiful interface that helps you get work done without getting in the way.

Supports: .odt, .docx, .doc, .pdf, .rtf

  • Draw shapes and diagrams, add text, charts, tables and links.
  • More user power with fields, FontWork and text rotation.
  • Comment and collaborate with others, then download as an image or pdf to insert into your documents and presentations.

Supports: .ods, .xlsx, .xls, .xlsm, .csv

  • Benefit from advanced formulas, pivot tables, HTML formula input, conditional formatting and data validation.
  • Create giant spreadsheets with up to 16k columns, add charts, sparklines and hyperlinks.
  • Powerful multi-column sort and filter.

Supports: .odp, .ppt, .pptx

  • Create a presentation to impress!
  • Easily design attractive master slides, add text, images, tables and SmartArt, then collaboratively edit with your colleagues.
  • Engage your audience with custom timings and transitions.
  • Present online or export to PDF.

Supports: .odg, .vsd, .vsdx

  • Draw shapes and diagrams, add text, charts, tables and links.
  • More user power with fields, FontWork and text rotation.
  • Comment and collaborate with others, then download as an image or pdf to insert into your documents and presentations.
