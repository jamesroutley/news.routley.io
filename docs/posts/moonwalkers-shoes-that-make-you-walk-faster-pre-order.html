Moonwalkers: Shoes that make you walk faster (pre-order)

Moonwalkers™ take your first steps into the future, walk at the speed of a run.  It’s like having a moving walkway on your feet. 

  • It’s not skating—it’s walking.

  • Moonwalkers™ move only when you do. This means you can go up and down stairs, step into mass transit, and confidently wait at the crosswalk.

Fast. Nimble. Safe.

Fast and effortless, get to your destination in less than half the time with a 250% increase in your walking speed.

Conquer the most demanding terrain

  • The 8-wheel patented drivetrain consists of overlapping wheels to simulate much larger diameter wheels to climb over uneven terrain.

Hands-free and convenient

Hands Free

Change modes with foot-driven gesture control for seamless transitions between SHIFT and LOCK modes. With no remote to worry about, you can let your hands be hands.

Easy On & Off

Moonwalkers™ take seconds to put on and take off, thanks to our magnetic buckles. Padded straps with massive adjustability allow them to fit any size shoe comfortably.

Compact & Secure
