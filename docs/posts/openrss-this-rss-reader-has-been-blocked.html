Open RSS offers feeds that are a much healthier alternative to the intrusive, algorithmic feeds on websites that harm and manipulate us. Our service continues to generate thousands of feeds, and we won't stop until every website has one. With RSS feeds, we can all finally follow web content on our terms, using feeds that only we control.

RSS feeds

Open RSS feeds track real-time content posted on any website that you can collect and subscribe to, and they come with many benefits

  • No algorithms

    Feed items are sorted chronologically by date and free of recommendation algorithms, moderation, self-prioritized ranking, and any other undesired curation

  • Read privately

    Embedded media scripts, link tracking, and other invasive content is stripped from feeds to avoid being tracked while reading

  • Full text

    Instead of just headlines or short summaries for each feed item, all relevant content is included in feeds, so you never have to leave your RSS reader

  • Active support

    To avoid feeds breaking due to sudden changes on the websites they rely on, all feeds are actively monitored for issues and resolved quickly, often without our users knowing there was ever a problem

  • No ads

    Excessive advertisements, promoted and "related" content—all designed to distract you and keep you reading forever—are removed from feeds, so you can stay focused

  • Fast updates

    All feeds are built for the fastest response times to get the most up-to-date content as soon as possible
