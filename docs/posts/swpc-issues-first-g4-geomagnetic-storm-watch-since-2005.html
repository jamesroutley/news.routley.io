SWPC Issues Its First G4 Watch Since 2005

published: Thursday, May 09, 2024 20:38 UTC

On Thursday, May 9, 2024, the NOAA Space Weather Prediction Center issued a Severe (G4) Geomagnetic Storm Watch.
