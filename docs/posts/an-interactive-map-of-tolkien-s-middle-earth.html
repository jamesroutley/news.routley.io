Show HN: An interactive map of Tolkien's Middle-earth

Welcome to Middle-earth

An interactive map of Tolkien’s Middle-earth, with events from across the legendarium plotted as markers.

  • Click markers to read about key events
  • Use the Legend panel to filter by book or toggle journey paths
  • Enable “Measure distance” in the Legend to measure between two points
  • Visit the Timeline page for a chronological view
