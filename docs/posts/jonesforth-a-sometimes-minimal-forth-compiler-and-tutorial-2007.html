The bit in an episode of I Only Listen To The Mountain Goats (S2E9) where John Darnielle says "People always ask me to play 'No Children' at weddings. Like -- No, I will not! I will absolutely not set the table for your wedding that way! If you wanna do that with a DJ, that's between you and the God that made you."

The idea of a scam where someone goes around suggesting to small towns that what would really solve their juvenile delinquency problems is a production of The Music Man. Of course you'll need to pay up front for the costumes, and allow four weeks for delivery...

Replies to a tweet about a time Rage Against The Machine, in a performance on British TV, went ahead and used profanity (in the line "F--- you, I won't do what you tell me") despite the producers' request that they not.
