Voxon: Real time interactive volumetric holograms

Welcome to the future

Voxon’s VLED technology creates real time interactive volumetric holograms using millions of points of light floating in 3D space. Captivating and immersive, these holograms can be viewed from 360 degrees so everyone can experience them from their own perspective.  What was once imagined in science fiction is now a reality. Welcome to the future of entertainment, communication and data visualization.

Products

The VX2 is Voxon’s next generation volumetric hologram technology - the result of years of engineering innovation and technological development. ​Compatible with standard 3D file formats and workflows, with the VX2 you can begin showcasing interactive volumetric holograms today.

Voxon VX2 

The VX2-XL delivers an unparalleled volumetric experience, with increased visual clarity and a larger display area that enables immersive, high-impact holographic content. Designed for commercial use, the VX2-XL is ideal for environments that require captivating, shared visual experiences, such as museums, entertainment venues, educational institutions, and corporate settings.
