Today is Y Combinator's 17th birthday

Today is Y Combinator's 17th birthday! Jessica and I were walking down the sidewalk right about here when we decided to start it.

It was originally called Cambridge Seed. But we realized the business would be national and didn't want to be tied to a regional name, so we changed it. At first the idea was seed funding with standardized terms. We stumbled upon the batch model a week or two later.

One thing that was there from the very beginning was the focus on founders, especially technical founders. This was back in the days when VCs would require such founders to hand over the job of CEO to some manager as a condition of funding. We'd been there and hated that.
