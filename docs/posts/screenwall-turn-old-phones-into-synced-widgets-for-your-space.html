Questions before you sign up?

Common things people want to know first.

Do I need to install an app on every screen?

No. Screens join from the browser with a QR code or PIN, which makes it much easier to reuse spare phones and tablets.

Will this work with old or mixed devices?

That is one of the main goals. ScreenWall is built for mixed walls of phones, tablets, and monitors, including older devices and legacy browsers where possible.

Can I try it without a big setup?

Yes. You can start with a few devices, create a small wall, and see whether the experience fits your space before investing more time.

What can I put on a wall?

You can place images, videos, GIFs, slideshows, live canvas feeds, and programmable canvas apps for widgets or art-driven visuals.

Do I need perfect matching hardware?

No. The product is meant for real-world leftovers: different screen sizes, bezels, orientations, and device generations.

Is ScreenWall already finished?

Not yet. ScreenWall is still in beta, which means the core experience is usable, but we are still improving stability, compatibility, and polish.
