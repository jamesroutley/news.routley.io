Singapore: A Local Walk (PM)

 

 

(This is a continuation from the previous post)

The question I have been asked many times is why I chose to stay at the Fullerton instead of the Fullerton Bay Hotel. The straightforward answer, as in most things, is that I do not like getting lost. I always like to know my start and end point, and I knew exactly where the Fullerton was located in relation to other places I had previously visited in this area.

The Fullerton is housed in a historic National Monument (Number 71, to be precise). The Fullerton Bay is ‘attached’ to a restored Clifford Pier. It is easy to get from one to the other via a dedicated underground passageway. I am certain that service at both are similar (it is outstanding at the Fullerton).

I decided to walk around the bay in the evening, to catch the Spectra Light Show at Marina Bay Sands. Here are the photos from my very pleasant walk.
