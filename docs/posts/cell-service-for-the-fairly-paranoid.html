  • Do I have to turn on Last-Mile Encrypted Texting? (iPhone users only)

    No, it's completely optional. However, we highly recommend turning it on. Without Last-Mile Encryption, group chats in your iPhone's Messages app that include Android members may become de-threaded, meaning messages are sent or received individually instead of as part of the group thread.

    We are working towards a long-term solution, but for now we recommend enabling Last-Mile Encryption to ensure your group chats work as expected. Last-Mile Encryption also keeps your SMS and MMS messages more secure. iMessages are not impacted as those are already end-to-end encrypted.
