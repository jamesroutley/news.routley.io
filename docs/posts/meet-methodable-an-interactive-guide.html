Meet Methodable: An Interactive Guide

I am releasing my latest human-programming tool: Methodable! And I’d love for you to try it out.

The rest of this post is presented as an interactive Methodable guide. If you have a nice chunk of time to go through the guide, that’s great; if not, feel free to check out the first few pages of the guide for a brief introduction. I’ll also publish sub-sections of the guide through this newsletter with additional technical commentary over the next few weeks.

Please send any feedback or suggestions to humanprogramming@substack.com or dnsosebee on Twitter. Also please reach out if you’d like to chat or get help using Methodable.

Methodable does not yet have a mobile interface, so please click the button below on your laptop or desktop computer (if you’ve opened Methodable before, see the note below).
