The Games that shaped you are disappearing

Remember those games that changed how you think about gaming?

Every year, more of them vanish forever – and with them, a piece of who we are.

Obsolete systems

Old games often can’t run on today’s computers. Without fixes, updates, or legal emulation, they’re left unplayable.

Lost source code

The original files and assets are often lost or damaged, making faithful restoration nearly impossible.

Expired Licenses

Rights expire or vanish in legal limbo, preventing beloved games from ever being re-released.

We made fixing this problem our mission.

For nearly two decades, we’ve brought forgotten classics back to life - testing, fixing, and adapting them for modern systems.

Because of this, millions of gamers can enjoy games from the '90s and '00s again.
