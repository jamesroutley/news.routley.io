While we wait for it all to come together, there are OTHER things worth being excited about, chief among them: A Very Good Tomato Sauce is now available at one of my favorite places to order groceries from, Farm to People. If you’re in the New York area, rejoice!

As told in A Little Newsletter, my last trip en route to Amsterdam wasn’t as peaceful as I fantasized it would be, but that said, while we were there, we did have a great time. It was relatively easy to get around with tiny prince in tow (for those interested: we traveled with the Cybex convertible stroller situation which is both a car seat and stroller in one, and both the airbnb and hotel we stayed in had cribs/pack and plays, so we didn’t need to bring our own).   

A number of you have asked for recommendations for a trip later this year or maybe next year or maybe just one day, so you’ll find the list below. Per usual, it’s a list compiled of other peoples suggestions and places we stumbled upon. For me, travel is best when you ask as many people as possible for their suggestions (friends, strangers, etc) and then ignore those suggestions when it’s too inconvenient to do otherwise– you might surprise yourself with what you find. 

I’ll say, we really loved Amsterdam. The parks, the weather, the people, the cafes– all a dream. While I was there for work (Something from Nothing is published in DUTCH can you believe it? And later this year……French. Stay tuned!), I was able to get in a few days of aimless strolling with my family. Max was not interested in figuring out how to navigate the bicycle culture with a toddler, so we walked everywhere (my preferred method of transit, to be honest). I was delighted at how totally doable that was, and yes, we did average over 20k steps a day, and yes, we DID make a daily comment about “getting our steps in.”
