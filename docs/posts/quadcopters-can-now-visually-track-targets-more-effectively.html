Quadcopters can now visually track targets more effectively

October 19, 2023 | Flying Machines

Researchers from NYU Tandon School of Engineering uploaded a paper on what they say is a new way to visually track targets using quadcopters.

Their unifying foundation model works efficiently on limited hardware and can precisely detect a range of objects from humans to pigeons. They even have a nice, and totally not terrifying shot of drones tracking humans running away. There will be no escape.
