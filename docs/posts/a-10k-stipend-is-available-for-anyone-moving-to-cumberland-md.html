A $10k stipend is available for anyone moving to Cumberland, MD

Considering moving to Cumberland? 

The City is offering a new program designed to attract families to live inside City limits.  The package, offering up to $20,000 is comprised of $10,000 in relocation cash, PLUS up to $10,000, dollar for dollar match, for approved renovations on an existing home, OR for a down payment on a newly constructed home within City limits.

Intrigued?  Click here to read about the Eligibility Requirements for the program and learn more about the terms and conditions of the program.

Want to make the move?  Apply here for the relocation program!  

The completed application form should be emailed to:  Ruth Davis-Rogers (ruth.davis-rogers@cumberlandmd.gov).
