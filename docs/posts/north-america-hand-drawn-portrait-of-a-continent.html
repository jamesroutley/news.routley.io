North America: Hand-Drawn Portrait of a Continent

North America: Portrait of a Continent was drawn completely by hand with color pencil and pen. It is a 5 x 4 foot (150 x 120cm) perspective projection of the region, spanning all of Canada, the USA, Mexico, Central America, Greenland, much of the Caribbean, and an inset of Hawaii. The detail is vast and practically unlimited, and includes 600 individual cities and towns. I drew it between May 2014 and February 2019.
