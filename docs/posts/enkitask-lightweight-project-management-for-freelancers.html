Show HN: EnkiTask: Lightweight Project Management for Freelancers

Agile-inspired project coordination meets unlimited collaboration. Our platform brings Scrum methodology to life, empowering teams of any size to work efficiently and communicate freely. No user limits mean you can involve everyone without breaking the bank.

Flexible, budget-friendly task management reimagined. We've ditched subscriptions in favor of a pay-for-task model. Purchase task packages that fit your workflow, and only spend when you're actively using the system. It's project management that adapts to your needs and your budget.

  • Unlimited projects. Create as many projects as you want, whenever you want. There are no limits or restrictions on the number of projects you can make. Whether you're working on personal tasks, professional endeavors, or creative pursuits, you have the freedom to bring all your ideas to life.
  • Unlimited Team Collaboration. As a project owner, you have the power to create teams and add as many teammates as you need, all completely free. There's no limit to the number of users you can include in each team or assign to each project, and there are no additional charges based on team size. Do not worry about per-user fees.
  • Unlimited Storage Store all your files and images without limits or costs. Our platform offers unlimited space for your project materials, allowing you to upload and share as much as you need. Whether it's documents, high-resolution photos, or large design files, you'll never run out of room or face additional charges.
  • AI-Generated changelogs Changelogs will be generated based on completed tasks, providing an accurate and up-to-date record of all the progress made. Ensure your team and stakeholders are always informed about the latest updates and progress..

Our system equips you with essential project management features to streamline your workflow. At its core, you'll find versatile boards including Backlog and Sprint views, allowing you to organize and prioritize tasks effectively. Track project progress with ease, set and monitor goals, and gain clear insights into your team's performance.
