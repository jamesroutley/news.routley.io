A deliberate practice app for guitar players who want to level up

for guitar players who want to level up

Practice using a smart metronome that captures metrics and turns them into actionable insights; paired with an effective practice method focusing on speed, endurance, accuracy, and adaptability.

  • No knowledge of music theory required
  • A library of exercises to import from, or create your own
  • Free to use. No ads. No signup
  • Works in any browser on PC & smartphone. No app download needed
  • Data is stored on your device. Export/Delete anytime.
  • Works in "Offline mode" (if the browser supports it)

Though Captrice aims for sustained progress, a 30 minutes practice session is enough to see the difference in your playing.

What makes captrice effective?

Features

Metronome

An ergonomic metronome, easily operated using keyboard shortcuts while playing guitar

Exercise builder

Build your repository of exercises. Organize them into collections

Tablature/Notation

Embed guitar tablature and music notation in exercises for quick reference

Library

Get access to a library of exercises covering speed-building drills, finger independence exercises, warmup routines and more.

Insights

Track your progress and get actionable insights through beautiful, intuitive visualizations

Sharing

Export/Import functionality to share exercises with your friends, students and audience

Use cases

Practice daily

Create/Import exercises based on scales, arpeggios, triads etc. Add some warm up exercises into the mix. Aim to practice them daily. Let the practice streaks chart fuel your motivation.

Build speed

Want to nail a fast guitar solo/riff?

Target weak spots

Work on specific guitar techniques such as two hand tapping, legato, sweep picking etc. Find an exercise from the library or create your own based on a tutorial video that works for you.

Beginner or expert, Captrice helps you improve by facilitating a game-changing practice framework
