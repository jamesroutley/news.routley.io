Crunch

Published on Jan 6th, 2025.

It’s been cold in New York these last few days and we’ve had some snow accumulate and stick.

The bedroom of the place I’m staying is on the ground level facing the street, meaning I can hear the footsteps of those who pass by. This morning there was a certain crunch of somebody’s steps in the snow that told me immediately it was a sub-freezing day. Anybody who grew up in a place with cold winters will know what I mean.

I love that crunch sound.
