Questions & answers

Which email provider(s) are Notion Mail compatible with?

Notion Mail integrates with Google and Gmail accounts.

When will Notion Mail be available on mobile devices?

The iOS app is coming soon! Notion Mail will be available on Android in 2025, too.

What languages are Notion Mail available in?

Notion Mail is currently available in English. We will support an additional 13 languages (just like Notion!) very soon.

Can I manage multiple emails accounts on Notion Mail?

Yes! You can use multiple Notion accounts to manage different emails. We currently do not offer a unified inbox that shows emails from all your accounts in one place.

What are Notion Mail's security standards?

Notion Mail is SOC2 certified and HIPAA compliant, just like Notion. It also respects the same terms and privacy policies you already trust. Learn more.
