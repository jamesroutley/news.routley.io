Penpot, Open Source Figma

Penpot is the first Open Source design and prototyping platform meant for cross-domain teams. Non dependent on operating systems, Penpot is web based and works with open web standards (SVG). For all and empowered by the community.

Meant for designers &

For cross-domain teams

Product features and capabilities meant for the different roles in the next-decade team. Say goodbye to the legendary pain of the design silo.

With love and Open Standards

Using SVG as no other prototyping tool does, Penpot files sport compatibility with most of the vectorial tools, are tech friendly and extremely easy to use in web.

Multiplatform

Being web based, Penpot is not dependent on operating systems or installations, you will only need to run a modern browser.

Open Source power

Built for the community and empowered by the community. Extreme adaptability: contributions can range from add-ons and plugins to core functionality.

Design

Design components at lightspeed

Create beautiful user interfaces in collaboration with all team members. Maintain consistency at scale with components, libraries and design systems.

Prototype

Interactive prototypes for everyone

Create rich interactions to mimic the product behaviour. Share to stakeholders, present proposals to your team and start user testing with your designs, all in one place.

Collaborate

Design might begin with you, but it's teamwork that makes it whole

All team members working simultaneously with real time design multiplayer and centralised comments, ideas and feedback right over the designs.

Feedback

One priceless conversation

One priceless conversation getting and providing feedback right over the designs and prototypes. Share a link to your design files or prototypes, and get feedback in context.
