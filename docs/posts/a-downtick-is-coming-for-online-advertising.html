A downtick is coming for online advertising

Obstacles are mounting against online-advertising giants, but the pain won’t be spread evenly.

Ahead of first-quarter reports coming from the industry’s major players over the next few weeks, Wall Street has taken on a notably cautious tone for the sector. The combined weight of rising inflation, continued supply-chain challenges, the war in Ukraine and changes last year to Apple ’s mobile operating system are all expected to take some toll on the sector.
