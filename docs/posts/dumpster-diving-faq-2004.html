Dumpster Diving FAQ (2004)

(A "dumpster," by the way, is the American word for the big metal garbage bin that is called a "skip" in the UK and Australia. Also it's been a few years since I wrote this, and now I do almost no dumpster diving because I'm based in Spokane where all the stores have trash compactors. Even in Seattle it seems like the best ones have dried up, but probably there are new good ones that I don't know about.)
