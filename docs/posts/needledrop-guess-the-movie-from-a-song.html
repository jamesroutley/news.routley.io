Show HN: NeedleDrop – Guess the movie from a song

About

After subjecting many friends and family to spontaneous ‘guess the movie’ sessions, it dawned on me that others might enjoy scratching this nostalgic trivia itch as well.

Inspired by Wordle and Framed, I cracked open my text editor and used ChatGPT to build out every chunk of functionality I envisioned. It’s truly amazing what you can learn and achieve with that tool.

  • Movie data sourced from TMDB
  • Most scene hints are from Shotdeck
  • No revenue or monetization planned. This is just for fun!

If you enjoyed this game fun or want to reach out, please give me a follow @wernah.

nee·dle·drop noun

A Needle Drop is when a pre-existing song is used by a film director to underscore a particular scene.

How do you play?

Each day a new Needle Drop challenge is released from a curated list†.

Guess the movie you think the Needle Drop was featured in. You’re allowed 4 guesses. A clue is revealed for each incorrect guess.

Share your score with friends for cinephile bragging rights.

† Sometimes Needle Drops appear in multiple movies. We’ve selected what we believe to be the first or most iconic instance of each Needle Drop.
