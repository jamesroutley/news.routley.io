Hello and welcome to Hell Gate. We are a subscriber-funded, worker-owned news outlet about New York City, named for the city’s sturdiest (and handsomest) bridge over its most treacherous currents.

The goal? To be trenchant, playful, outraged, irreverent, and useful to our readers; deeply skeptical of power but stubbornly idealistic. And never a chore to read.

I’ve been a subscriber since they first started and have never regretted it. They stole my heart with Where Does Upstate Begin If You Are Traveling at the Speed of Light?
