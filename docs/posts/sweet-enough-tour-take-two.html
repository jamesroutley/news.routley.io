Hello to the amazing residents of Austin, Nashville, Minneapolis, Toronto, Montreal, and The Boston Metropolitan Area! I had such a great time on tour in the spring but simply not enough time to go to all the places, so I’m extremely pleased to announce I’ll be bopping through your fine cities in November for the second part of my Sweet Enough book tour. That’s right, I’ll be HOLDING a MICROPHONE on STAGE again– a thrill for us all!

We’ll chat about Sweet Enough, all things baking, cooking, book making, and, of course, living, laughing and loving. There will be merch, there will be books and there will be opportunities to get yours signed. I can’t wait to get back out there, see if steak tartare is still on every menu everywhere, get my favorite hard shell tacos in Austin (De Nada I’m coming for you), and earn some Delta SkyMiles. If you have recommendations where to eat in any of those places, please let me know in the comments (when I’m “on the road” I especially like soup? Just a little bit about me!).

The first time around, tickets sold out much faster than I thought they would, so if you want to go (and I want you to go!), I’d recommend scooping them sooner than later– today, even! And, if you’re a paying subscriber, you CAN, by scrolling down for the presale access code below the paywall.
