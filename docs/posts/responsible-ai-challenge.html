Responsible AI Challenge

by Mozilla Builders

AI is changing everything. Now is the time to develop it responsibly.

Live event: May 31, 2023 in San Francisco, CA

Apply now

Your opportunity

Apply

Tell us about the generative AI project you want to develop and how you’ll develop it responsibly. You don’t have to have it all figured out (that’s why the application is short!) — we want to see how you think.

Become a finalist

10 teams will become finalists and be invited to an in-person event in San Francisco with some of the brightest thinkers, technologists, ethicists and business leaders to help you get your idea off the ground and unlock a variety of technology resources.

Win big

Attend workshops and talks about algorithmic bias, evaluation and transparency. Refine your pitch and get an opportunity to win a cash prize. Plus, get access to mentorship from leaders in the industry and meet like-minded innovators.

Things you should know

The challenge is open to everyone regardless of geographical location* but you must be present for the in-person event.

The key dates

  • March 30th: Applications open
  • April 20th: Applications close
  • May 8th: Top 10 finalists announced
  • May 31st: In-person event for top 10 finalists

The prizes

  • 1st prize: $25,000
  • 2nd prize: $15,000
  • 3rd prize: $10,000

The requirements

  • Early-stage projects only
  • Make sure you have the rights to any IP, code or data in use
  • Individuals and teams can apply
  • No cap on number of teammates
  • Modest travel and accommodation reimbursements will be provided for finalists
  • One winner for each prize

*Terms and Conditions

How projects will be evaluated

Concept innovation

How is your project unique in its approach and how does it solve a specific problem or need in society?

Technical implementation

What technology stack and datasets will you leverage to make your AI solution work?

Responsible AI

Does your project consider things like agency, accountability, privacy, fairness, and safety? How does it address our Responsible AI Guidelines?
